# POCKET CITY 2 — VISUAL REFERENCE ARCHIVE (saved 7/23/26)

Paolo sent these as the reference standard for Bohemia's iso building art, after
the district-hero v1 (and my sight-unseen v2) missed: "look at pocket city 2
buildings online and what they look like... they were all ICONIC you could tell
what the building was and the purpose of it."

**These are a MOOD BOARD / reference, not assets to copy.** They belong to Code
Brew Games (Pocket City 2). We study them for the READABILITY + FORM LANGUAGE and
derive OUR OWN rules — see `records/BOHEMIA_POCKET_CITY_STYLE_REFERENCE.md` (the
style bible). Kept in git because the session container is ephemeral and GIT IS
THE MEMORY; a reference we only glanced at is a reference we lost.

## What's here (66 screenshots, wiki cards + live gameplay)
- **Wiki building cards** (IMG_3944-3996): the catalogue thumbnails by category —
  Water (water tower, sewage drain, sewage lagoon, water treatment plant),
  Power (small/large/hydro/fusion/wind/solar/waste power plants, biomass),
  Education (schools, college, university, library, museum, historical center,
  technology tower, robotics factory, space center), Resources (lumber mill,
  farm, greenhouse, mines, mills, factories, docks), Commercial (bank, billboard),
  Unique (town hall, animal shelter, mayor's mansion/pool, news station, obelisk,
  monolith), Mega Projects (amusement park, airport, zoo, stadium, commerce
  towers, eco research lab, pyramid city, space elevator).
- **Live gameplay** (IMG_3997-4022): the real rendered city, day AND night —
  residential streets (colored-roof houses + white picket fences + trees),
  downtown (mid-rise towers, storefronts with awnings + billboards), and the
  industrial belt (red-brick factories + smokestacks, gray steel mill, orange
  ore-processing plant, water-treatment tanks, blue crystal ore piles).

The live gameplay shots are the truest reference — the wiki thumbnails are tiny.
The style bible distills both.
