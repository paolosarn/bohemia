=== BOHEMIA DRESS CODE BY RANK — MECHANISM SHIPPED (7/21/26) ===
Paolo picked the next factory lane: dress codes, starting with color.
His rule, verbatim: "rookies of the faction can wear whatever as long as
half their body is a certain color... veteran faction members actually
have to wear most of the clothes we give them."

=== WHAT SHIPPED (mechanism mine, contents his) ===
engine/bohemia_dress.js gained two dressing modes, wired through a new
outfitForRanked() that dressAll() calls with an optional agent.rank:
- ROOKIE: the same free draw as every agent today, nudged region-by-region
  (heaviest body-surface weight first: torso, legs, head, feet) toward
  >=50% faction-color coverage, using only canon items that are already
  legal for that layer. If the wardrobe carries no matching-color item in
  a region, that region is skipped -- never fabricated.
- VETERAN: the free draw, then every layer Paolo's kit names is FORCED to
  his exact canon garment (hashed pick among his listed options). Layers
  he didn't name stay a free draw. A free-drawn outer can never cover a
  kit-governed base (fixed same session: caught a stray coat hiding a
  forced base color in the visual proof, patched before shipping).
- "Half the body" is ONE number everywhere: body-surface weights derived
  from the same part-pixel geometry the clothing gate already uses to
  prove garment shapes (torso 0.437 / legs 0.322 / head 0.161 / feet
  0.080).
FACTION_COLOR and FACTION_VETERAN_KIT both ship EMPTY -- no faction's
color or veteran garments are ruled here. An agent with no rank or no
ruling for their faction dresses exactly as the population always has;
zero regression (LIFE gate still 24/24 unchanged).

VERIFIED: gates/dress_gate.js grew from 9 to 23 checks (tables empty, rank
functions inert with no ruling, rookie coverage >=50% across 40 seeds with
a throwaway test color, an unmatchable color degrades to legal-but-
uncovered rather than inventing a garment, veteran kit forced every seed,
unspecified layers still free, the cover-up fix proven both directions).
Visual proof rendered: 10 rookies (all clearing 76-92% coverage, no two
identical) against 6 veterans (locked, identical base/legs/feet/head every
single seed).

Law: laws/BOHEMIA_ADDENDUM_DRESS_CODE_BY_RANK_7_21_26.md.

=== SECOND PASS (7/21, same day): SIX FACTIONS RULED FOR REAL ===

red/maroon, CARAVANS stays tan (blends with the desert on purpose), CHURCH
gold distinct from MOB's gold, MOB gold STRIPES not solid gold, COLORFUL a
literal rainbow with no boring neutrals, and a general realism note: real
people don't wear a single solid color head to toe.

MECHANISM grew three real modes (family/stripe/rainbow) plus a realism fix
(nudge order: torso, then a small tell -- feet or head -- LEGS ONLY as a
last resort, never a matching-separates costume) plus a generalized
no-stray-cover-up rule (an unforced outer or hat can no longer hide a
color/pattern correctly forced onto base or face underneath).

every "vivid" item was red-brown family, so COLORFUL was literally

NEW mistake: a violet sash and a first magenta pick both tripped PURPLE
RESERVATION (r+b-over-g reads as purple-family even off pure hue) -- the
rose swap clears it. Wardrobe: 195 canon items.
