BOHEMIA COMBAT — WEAPON LETHALITY RESEARCH (7/21/26)
======================================================
Paolo's ask: "do big brain research on how many people survive shootouts,
especially if hit lethally... my approval will be the floor."

THE REAL-WORLD NUMBERS (NTDB — National Trauma Data Bank studies, via
web research; sources at bottom)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Overall gunshot-wound fatality rate across all severities: ~22%
  (~78% of people who get shot survive SOME wound).
- Handgun-inflicted wounds: in-hospital mortality 44-50% when the
  round is deliberately placed (suicide-attempt data, closest analog
  to an aimed shot).
- Rifles: found to be the MOST lethal of common firearms; rifle
  victims are more likely to die before ever reaching an ER.
- Shotguns: counter-intuitively had the LOWEST mortality of the
  firearms studied (31.9%) in that dataset — but that number covers
  buckshot-spread wounds at real-world engagement distances, not a
  deliberately aimed point-blank vital hit, which is what our dial's
  KILL zone represents.
- Headshots specifically: 10-15% survival (85-90% lethal) — the
  closest real analog to our dial's KILL zone, which is explicitly the
  vital/headshot-tier hit.

THE FLOOR I'M PROPOSING (a game-design lever, not a literal sim —
real data doesn't cleanly support "pistol always spares, shotgun
always kills," but Paolo's ask is a GAMEPLAY IDENTITY lever: pistol
for a pacifist route, shotgun for finality)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
On a KILL-zone dial result, chance the hit is INSTANTLY LETHAL
(skips the DOWNED state entirely) rather than dropping the target
DYING at 1 HP:
  PISTOL  : 0%    — never auto-kills on its own; always downs. The
                    pacifist's tool, exactly as he asked.
  SMG     : 15%   — mostly downs, occasional double-tap finishes it.
  RIFLE   : 35%   — rifles are the most lethal round in the real data;
                    a killshot with one is a real risk of no mercy.
  SHOTGUN : 100%  — his exact ruling: shotgun killshots are dead-dead,
                    period, no downed state.
This is the FLOOR — SMG/rifle numbers are the ones with the most
room to move on his say-so.
