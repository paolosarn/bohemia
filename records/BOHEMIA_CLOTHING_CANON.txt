--- THE HEADWEAR FRAMEWORK: DOME, NOT PYRAMID (7/19/26) — Paolo: "why are the
    beanies pyramid shaped... glued to the top looking like a triangle. Don't
    delete those beanies, but understand the headwear framework." ---
ROOT CAUSE: hats followed the skull's per-row outline, and skulls NARROW toward
the crown -- so every hat inherited the taper and read as a pyramid. Pixel-art
convention (researched): a hat is a rounded BLOCK at full head width, flat top,
1px corner rounding.
THE FRAMEWORK: genHat fit:'dome' (DEFAULT) = block at the head's max width from
top to hat line. fit:'skull' = the taper-following variant, KEPT because Paolo
kept the pointy beanies (CHARCOAL/RUST WATCH CAP + OLIVE FIELD CAP stay exactly
as cooked, tagged fit:'skull'). Gate: rounded synthetic skull + DOME LAW check
(13 checks) -- a future pyramid goes red.
COOKING now 6: the 3 kept pointy + 3 dome-framework (STORM KNIT CAP, SAND KNIT
CAP, SLATE WORK CAP).
