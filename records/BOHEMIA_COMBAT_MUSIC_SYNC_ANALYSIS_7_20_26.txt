THE VERDICT
-----------
THE KICK IS THE HEARTBEAT. It is the one physical layer every combat song
shares, and its anchor hits are steps 0 and 8 — beats 1 and 3, the
downbeats. The bass is technically more step-0-consistent across the full
catalog but it is a pitched layer; the kick is the thing your chest feels.

WHAT GOT BUILT ON THIS (v24 + v25, same turn)
---------------------------------------------
- KICK-LOCK (v24): the dead-eye dial no longer pulses on the bare 120
  grid. The ember kill band pump and a new rim flash ride the CURRENT
  SONG'S actual kick pattern (FAC().kick, override-aware for studio
  pushes, fallback [0,8]). You SEE the kick you HEAR.
- EAR-LOCK (v25): research consensus (NecroDancer auto-sync, Rhythm Quest
  devlogs): phone speakers deliver audio TENS OF MS after the game clock
  (AudioContext outputLatency). The pulse clock now shifts by the
  device's own measured output latency, automatically. This is the
  usual root cause of "the rhythm feels off" on mobile.
- STILL ON THE SHELF [PENDING Paolo]: a tap-to-calibrate setting (the
  NecroDancer approach) for players whose bluetooth speakers lie about
  their latency; and whether kick-timed shots should ever EARN anything
  mechanically (currently feel-only — timing windows unchanged).
