## THE RULING (LOCKED)

1. LAKE MEAD IS HEALTHY BY GAME START. Not dying, not on a countdown. The
   bathtub ring is SHRINKING (the lake is coming back up). This is the canon
   state when the player arrives, roughly a decade post-crash.

2. THE DAM WORKS. Hoover Dam generates real power. Vegas has real pumped water.
   The city is a FUNCTIONING OASIS, not a straw stuck in a drying puddle. This
   is why crossing the killing distance to reach Vegas is worth it: light,
   water, and life in the middle of a dead desert.

3. STORY BEATS SCIENCE ON DIRECT CONFLICT (authorial principle, Paolo's call).
   Where real-world hydrology says the dam should be near dead pool, the world
   of Bohemia says otherwise, and the world wins. Paolo, in spirit: "if that
   breaks actual world science, whatever, that's just how I feel and that's
   okay." Record this as a governing principle, not a one-off: Bohemia is
   grounded in the real until Paolo authors an exception, and his exceptions
   are canon. Ground first, then defer to his ruling.

## WHY THE RULING IS ALSO GROUNDED (the demand-collapse math)

Paolo's reasoning is a real second-order consequence of the die-off, not only
an authored fix:
- The single largest water use in the Las Vegas Valley is OUTDOOR landscaping
  (lawns, fountains, resort vanity, golf irrigation). The valley already
  recycles nearly all INDOOR water back to Lake Mead for reuse credits, so
  indoor use barely counts against the allocation. Consumptive use is
  dominated by the vanity load.
- Post-crash Bohemia kills the vanity load entirely (no lawns, no fountains, no
  vanity water, the one surviving golf course is a Karen-held exception) AND
  removes ~90% of the population (the locked ~2.2M-of-2.3M-gone figure).
- Demand therefore craters far below the shrunken supply. A recovering lake is
  the expected result, not a miracle.
- THE AUTHORED CHERRY (not strictly required): in Bohemia the Colorado Rockies
  watershed runs a little wetter (more snowpack), nudging supply up too. Belt
  and suspenders. The demand collapse alone would carry it.

## WHAT THIS SUPERSEDES

The 7/18 break's "dying dam / water runs out in act 3 / mob rations a failing
trickle" framing is DEAD. There is no water-scarcity countdown. Do not build a
doom clock on the lake level.

  through abundance instead of scarcity.

  abundance. Someone's hand is on the dam, the pumps, and the grid, and that
  hand decides which blocks live well. This is the LIGHT=TERRITORY / 12%-lit
  law with real water behind it. Who that hand belongs to is [PENDING Paolo,

  stays precious because it is territory and comfort and pump-work, not because

- CASINO GOES ANALOG (still live, independent of this ruling): the last casino
