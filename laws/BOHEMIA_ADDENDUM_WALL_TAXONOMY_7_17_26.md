# BOHEMIA — ADDENDUM: WALL TAXONOMY (Paolo ruling, 7/17/26)

Locked in chat while opening the batch-2 wall judging, verbatim intent:
"This is for the walls of suburb communities, different than like building
wall, so keep that in mind."

## THE RULING
There are TWO wall classes and they never share a pool:

1. **PERIMETER / COMMUNITY WALLS** — the walls that enclose a suburb cell
   (CELL-IS-PLOT + WALLED SUBURBS, 7/14). Standalone runs, min 2 tiles tall,
   85% tan / 15% original, drawn from BOHEMIA_PERIMETER_WALL_POOL. The WALL
   PICKER batches (batch 1 = W26-W37 approved 7/14; batch 2 = the remaining
   48, judged 7/17+) feed THIS pool and ONLY this pool.

2. **BUILDING WALLS** — the walls of structures (houses, commercial, the
   house factory's parts). Different pool, different pipeline (HOUSE_FACTORY
   and the tilepool's TAN WALL 85/15 law). Wall-picker verdicts NEVER leak
   into building-wall pools.

## CONSEQUENCE FOR THE PIPELINE
- Batch-2 UP verdicts -> tan factory -> PERIMETER_WALL_POOL widening. Nothing
  else. Any future building-wall judging is its own batch with its own tool,
  labeled as such.
- Any tool that surfaces wall candidates MUST state on its face which class
  is being judged (the 7/17 picker was updated the same turn).

MECHANISM-MINE / CONTENTS-PAOLO'S: the taxonomy is his; the pools stay split
by machine.
