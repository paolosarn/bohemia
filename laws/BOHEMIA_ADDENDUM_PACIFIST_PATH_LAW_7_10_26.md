# BOHEMIA — PACIFIST PATH LAW (locked 7/10/26 by Paolo)

## THE LAW
A full pacifist playthrough must be possible from start to finish. You can go the
ENTIRE game without killing anyone if you choose. BUT pacifist does NOT mean safe.
The world still tries to shoot you, capture you, eat you, whatever. Choosing peace
is opting out of KILLING, not opting out of DANGER.

## WHAT THIS REQUIRES (non-lethal is its own real mechanic set, not an absence)
- **Run-away button.** A real, always-available verb to flee an encounter. Escaping
  is a legitimate resolution, not a failure. (Wires to the I-move-you-move grid:
  run = two cells per beat, already filed.)
- **Dodge / evade mini-game.** A separate survival mini-game to survive incoming
  fire WITHOUT firing back — dodging bullets, breaking line of sight, weathering an
  assault until you can escape or de-escalate. This is the peaceful counterpart to
  the Dead Eye Dial. The player who never kills still needs a skill expression
  under fire.
- **Non-lethal resolutions** across the game: talk down, buy off, sneak past,
  out-position, use faction standing, surrender-and-escape, etc. Every major
  confrontation needs at least one non-killing route.

## DEAD EYE DIAL'S PLACE (unchanged)
The Dead Eye Dial is the tool for when you WANT your enemies dead — full lethal
playstyle. It is NOT the only verb in the game. It remains the current priority
for the combat build-out (killing all other edits first), but the pacifist verbs
(run, dodge/evade, de-escalate) are now locked as required companions to it, to be
built out when Paolo turns to them. No damage before the Dead Eye dial ever still
holds.

## DESIGN TEETH (peaceful is HARD, not easy — from the Quest Vault)
- **Undertale mercy-is-harder law:** the coexist/"Respect" path costs MORE — more
  resources, more turns on the clock, more skill under fire — not less. Sparing is
  earned, never a soft default.
- **Deus Ex multi-solution:** talk / sneak / evade / economy / faction standing are
  all valid keys to the same locked problem.
- **Dead Eye no-instakill design** already supports a world where not-killing is a
  real, mechanical choice rather than a menu toggle.

## THE THREE PLAYSTYLES THIS SERVES
Reinforces Liberate / Respect / Become. A player can carry the whole dynasty on the
Respect (coexist) path — the game must fully support the hand that never pulls a
lethal trigger, while the world keeps coming for them anyway.

## STATUS
