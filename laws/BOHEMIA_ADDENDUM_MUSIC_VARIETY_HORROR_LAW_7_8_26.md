# BOHEMIA ADDENDUM: MUSIC VARIETY LAW + HORROR DIRECTION (Paolo, 7/8/26)

## PAOLO'S CRITIQUE (verbatim direction)
"be more horror creepy post apocalyptic final fantasy and to not have them
all sound the same. new voices but u keep reusing some of the same
elements man."

## THE HONEST DIAGNOSIS (filed so it never repeats)
The NEW VOICES LAW was being honored in name only: new voice names, new
tunings, but the same FIVE synthesis skeletons recycled (detuned osc stack
into lowpass, FM pair, LFO-on-filter, vibrato, slow-attack pad), and the
same musical defaults (minor-pent scales, roots 43-52, kick on 0/8, half
feel). Different clothes, same body.

## STANDING DIRECTION DEFAULT (Paolo 7/8, LOCKED)
POST-APOCALYPTIC FINAL FANTASY HORROR CREEPY is the default direction for
every music session and every cook going forward, no invocation needed.
The full paste-able cook prompt ships as BOHEMIA_MUSIC_COOK_PROMPT.txt.

## MUSIC VARIETY LAW (LOCKED)
1. Every batch must introduce synthesis TOPOLOGIES the catalog has never
   used, not just new parameter values on old skeletons.
2. No two songs in one batch may share scale + feel + kick pattern.
3. The kill-reason on a thumbs-down is direction gold; Claude asks for one
   word when it is not obvious.

## THE HORROR KIT (built into synthV 7/8, first uses in the catalog)
- NOISE BUFFERS: wind, breath, pressure, scrapes (chapelbreath,
  pressurefront).
- NOISE-EXCITED RESONATOR plucks: wire physics without feedback
  (wireharp, boneshuffle). NOTE 7/8: originally shipped as karplus-strong
  feedback loops, which never died and stacked into a screech wall that
  hit Paolo's ears. SCREECH LAW locked same turn: no feedback loops in
  any voice ever, gate-enforced (_newvoice_gate.js), plus a brickwall
  limiter now guards the master chain.
- RING MODULATION: inharmonic metal, golden-ratio modulator (neonhowl).
- WAVESHAPER DISTORTION: the first overdrive in the game (jackpotghost).
- RANDOM PITCH-DRIFT CURVES: notes that never hold still (ghostvox), and
  a music box that sometimes plays the note WRONG (gonebox).
- DISSONANCE AS MATERIAL: semitone cluster organs (cryptorgan), minor-2nd
  shimmer (nurserydust), TRITONE drones (emptyfloorhum), phrygian and
  tritone scales, irregular kicks ([0,9], [0,7,11], [0,5,8,13]).
- HEARTBEAT SUB: lub-dub pitch-dropping pairs (heartbeatsub).

## HORROR BATCH 6 (all UNJUDGED, NEW badges)
THE CHOIR THAT STAYED: dead chapel, semitone organ clusters under a choir
voice that drifts off pitch, breath moving through the room.
MUSIC BOX FOR THE GONE: plucked-wire lullaby under a music box that
occasionally lands wrong, minor-second dust up high.

## HOW PAOLO STEERS THE COOKS (filed as the working interface)
1. SCENE IMAGES, one line ("a chapel where the choir kept singing after
   everyone died") = the strongest input, writes the whole song.
2. REFERENCES: name a track or a scene from anything, Claude decodes its
   machinery, never copies it.
3. KILL REASONS on downs: one word per corpse.
