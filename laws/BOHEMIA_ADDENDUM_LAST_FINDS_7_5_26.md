## 1. THE ARSENAL (the biggest find of the session)
Real, verified: Battlefield Vegas, a 5-acre military complex ONE BLOCK off
the strip behind Circus Circus. The facts:
- The largest collection of publicly fire-ready firearms in the UNITED
  STATES: several hundred to 2,000 guns, hundreds fully automatic, from
  WWI to modern NATO. Miniguns. A 20mm Vulcan cannon. Flamethrowers.
  Mortars. Grenade launchers.
- WORKING ARMOR: the largest privately-owned main battle tank in America
  (an M60A1 that actually FIRES its 105mm), a drivable Abrams-replica on a
  Chieftain hull, APCs with mounted .50 cals, 30+ military vehicles.
- Staffed 82% by combat VETERANS. Plus a 200-acre desert training
  facility outside town.
Read that back in hell-years terms: one block from the strip sits the most
militarily significant civilian site in the valley, an armory bigger than
most national guard depots, run by people who know how to use every piece
of it. Whoever holds the Arsenal on day one of the crash decides what the
first year of Bohemia looks like. This is not set dressing, this is why a
faction exists, or why one died.
[PENDING: who holds it at game start, what is left in it 10 years on, and

## 2. THE HELICOPTER FLEET
Real: Vegas hosts some of the largest civilian tour helicopter fleets
anywhere (Maverick and peers), dozens of airframes flying strip and Grand
Canyon runs daily. Post-crash: the only civilian air power in the region,
strangled by the fuel problem (jet fuel outlasts gasoline but not by a
decade). Ten years in: grounded fleets as parts treasure, or one faction

## 3. THE FIBER BACKBONE (the Amalgamation's arteries)
Grounded via the fortress's own origin story: Switch exists in Vegas
because of Enron's secret fiber plans, the long-haul fiber trunks running
along the I-15 corridor. Proposal: a fifth underground system, a thin
line under the spine from the CA border to the data fortress. The wire
the portraits traveled in on. Visually: one thread in the UNDER view that

## 4. THE SURVEILLANCE GRID (the cameras that fed the portraits)
Vegas casinos are among the most surveilled private spaces on earth,
thousands of cameras per property, facial recognition, decades of
footage. For a game whose antagonist is built from uploaded data
portraits, the casino surveillance ecosystem is where the raw material of
the Amalgamation came from. The cameras watched everyone, and then the
crash came, and the watching became something else. Map-level: a flag on
casino/resort/strip cells at most; the weight is lore and drill-in.

## 5. THE EOC (where the government made its last stand)
Real: Clark County runs an Emergency Operations Center, the room built
for hurricanes-of-the-desert, mass events, grid failures. It would have
been the last place the old civilization functioned, phones ringing until
they didn't. One cell near the civic block, a ruin with a story. [PENDING]
