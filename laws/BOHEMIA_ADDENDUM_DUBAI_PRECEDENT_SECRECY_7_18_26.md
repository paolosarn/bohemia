## LOCKED THIS TURN

1. THE AMALGAMATION IS A SHADOW SECRET, NOT PUBLIC KNOWLEDGE. The player barely
   knows what it is even by the END of Act 2, and only barely. Act 3 is the true
   explosion of the Amalgamation coming forward in the story. Maybe a couple of
   the player's companions know something. It is NOT widespread in the world's
   common knowledge. How it acts in the shadows is its own topic, built as we go.
   (This restates and reinforces the locked threat-logic model, it does not
   change it.)

2. THE DUBAI NUKE HAS A CAUSE, AND THE CAUSE IS THE POINT: the Amalgamation
   nuked Dubai because its secret was let out too quickly, to too many people.
   Dubai is the PRECEDENT, the proof of what the Amalgamation does when
   containment fails. It is not merely a mystery to solve; it is the demonstrated
   stakes. As the player nears the secret, they are walking the road Dubai
   walked, and Dubai is where that road ends.

## THE HOLE + GROUNDED RESOLUTION ([PENDING] where noted, not Paolo's words yet)

A nuke is the loudest act on earth, the opposite of secrecy. It only protects a
secret if the AUTHOR stays hidden while the event is public.
- RESOLUTION (proposed, [PENDING Paolo]): the world knows Dubai died; the world
  does NOT know the Amalgamation did it, or that the Amalgamation exists. The
  destruction carries a COVER STORY drawn from the collapse-era chaos already in
  canon (the Taiwan war, a reactor, a rival state). The atrocity is in daylight;
  the hand is secret. This is exactly how real deniable operations work, and it
  resolves the collapse addendum's open "hidden cabal vs in-daylight enabling"
  hole: Dubai is BOTH. What the player uncovers in Act 3 is not that a city died
  (everyone knows that) but that the official history of it is a lie.
- WHY DUBAI (proposed, [PENDING Paolo]): real Dubai is the ultra-rich playground
  and tax haven, exactly where "buy your way out of death" money and the
  re-embodiment tech would concentrate. Dubai reading as a NODE of that tech,
  where the secret pooled among the wealthy and then leaked, grounds why the burn
  landed there and not another megacity. Paolo has not said this; flagged only.
