## THE WORLD (locked)
- CRASH ~2050. Game starts almost 10 years later (~2060). It was a multi-causal
  ECONOMIC collapse, not a war or a bomb. The DOLLAR died. The economy runs on
  MEDICINE, ELECTRICITY, and RESOURCES.
- CIVILIZATION HELD ON BY A THREAD. Never a total blackout. Most industries and
  companies collapsed, stores barely alive, a huge number died, but power and
  technology persisted, strained. BROWNOUTS are the texture, not darkness.
- VEGAS IS A FUNCTIONING OASIS. Lake Mead is healthy and rising (die-off killed
  the vanity water demand). What people love and cross the desert for is the
  POWER and the WATER. FOOD is the weak leg, answered by HYDROPONICS at larger
  scale than earlier canon assumed (desert soil is useless for crops).
- POWER AND WATER ARE A HOUSE OF CARDS held by SHARE: dam + solar grid + pumps,
  no single owner, control proportional to each faction's share, and the Network
  skims covertly. It is interdependent and precious, but NOT one-bomb fragile.
- KINDNESS IS GLIMPSES, NOT UTOPIA. Scattered good in the fallout, survivors'
  stories of someone being kind, not a permanent peace.

## THE AMALGAMATION (locked)
- It is an INTERNATIONAL, DISTRIBUTED internet data service (like Claude), not a
  machine in a Vegas basement. You cannot kill it by unplugging local hardware.
  The Network's Vegas servers are its local NODE, not its brain.
- It is the ruins of the immortality bubble: PROJECT ANGEL sold digital
  immortality built from the data people fed their platforms. It is NOT a true
  consciousness upload, it is the most sophisticated SIMULATION of you. It formed
  by accident when millions of these portraits merged over decades.
- It is CUSTODIAL and it kills for ONE reason only: threat to its own survival
  and secrecy. Not power, not ambition. Fear. Threat scales with PROXIMITY TO
  THE SECRET, not player power. Rule the whole city and never look under it, and
  it never touches you.
- Its blind spot is the UNRECORDED self: face-to-face trust, tunnel talk, the
  things nobody ever posted. It can model everything recorded and nothing that
  was not.
- DUBAI is the precedent: the only post-crash nuke, when the secret spread too
  fast to too many. It got away clean and NOBODY KNOWS how the nuke went up. The
  unexplained horror is the point; do not over-explain it.
- It is a SHADOW SECRET. The player barely knows what it is even by the end of
  Act 2. Act 3 is its explosion into the story.

## THE THREE ENDINGS (THE canon; supersedes the old six-ending list)
The endgame branches three ways off the point of no return. (Paolo's words in
parentheses.)
1. LIBERATE (take it over and pull Bohemia free). Bohemia becomes a BASTION,
   locally free of the Amalgamation: you cut the strings, stop the nuke, and
   warn the rest of the world in the way Dubai failed to. You do NOT destroy the
   Amalgamation globally; you save one valley, and that is enough. WORLD = FREED.
2. RESPECT (respect it and let it continue). Coexistence. It stops the harvest
   and cools its appetite, the living let it exist. A grave that thinks, at
   peace. WORLD = COEXISTENCE.
3. BECOME (join it / take its power). Take the stewardship and become the god,
   kind or hard. The game does not punish this with failure, it damns it with
   success, because the corruption is made of kindnesses. WORLD = ASCENDED.

SUPERSEDED: the older GDD v2 six-ending framing (Liberation / Surrender /
Compromise / Unknown / Nuked / Space) is FOLDED into these three. The three are
canon. Any file still reading "six endings" is stale.

## THE FINALE (how it plays)
Act 3 is the explosion. The player fights toward the core while the broadcast
that could free the valley goes out in real time, and it cannot fight on two
fronts at once. PART OF THE BOSS IS PREVENTING ANOTHER NUCLEAR STRIKE: the Dubai
move returns as a clock, and stop-versus-redirect is the last moral choice, which
writes the final line of the monument. Which ending you land (Liberate / Respect
/ Become) is a function of the whole playthrough.
