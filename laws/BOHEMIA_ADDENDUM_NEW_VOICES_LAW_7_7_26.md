# BOHEMIA ADDENDUM: NEW VOICES LAW (Paolo, 7/7/26, LOCKED)

## THE LAW
1. AMENDED 7/8 (Paolo: "im not opposed to old voices touching the new
   voices i did want more diverse skeletons though"): old rack voices MAY
   appear in new songs, the rack is a living orchestra. The teeth are
   DIVERSITY: every new song still BIRTHS at least one newly synthesized
   voice (ledgered to its owner song), and the MUSIC VARIETY LAW (new
   synthesis topologies every batch, no two songs in a batch sharing
   scale+feel+kick) is the hard rule. The palette still only grows.
2. ALL NEW VOICES ARE POST-APOCALYPTIC FINAL FANTASY THEMED, always
   (Paolo, same day). Ruined grandeur: brass fanfares gone to rust, choir
   and bell liturgy, hymnal pads, the FF orchestral soul filtered through
   collapse. Voice NAMES carry it too.
3. GRAVEYARD IS FINAL (Paolo, same day): no remakes, ever. If it got
   graveyarded it was shit. Dead songs stay dead; the answer to a dead
   slot is a FRESH song with fresh voices. Standing directive: keep
   cooking fresh vibes, the game needs volume.

## FIRST ENFORCED BATCH (7/7/26, retroactively brought to law)
The three new songs cooked earlier today reused rack voices, illegal under
the law, rewired same day onto nine newly synthesized voices:
- THRONE OF STATIC: ruinbrass (detuned saw stack, slow rust-swell filter),
  staticfanfare (square+saw unison with a pitch-blip attack),
  kingsdust (dark triangle pad over a sub drone).
- CHOIR FOR THE MACHINE: machinechant (formant bandpass squares with LFO
  drift), foundrybell (FM bell, 2.76 ratio, long industrial decay + 3.01
  partial), serverchoir (breathy detuned saw choir, highpassed).
- FLOWERS ON THE FREEWAY: weedbloom (soft triangle pluck with closing
  filter + sub support), overpasslight (vibrato sine bell + octave
  shimmer), freewayhaze (warm triangle pad with slow filter wobble).

## ENFORCEMENT
_newvoice_gate.js: every new song's instrument set must be implemented in
synthV AND be unique to that song (exactly one data reference in the
catalog). Runs with the permanent gate set every music ship. VOICE LEDGER
lives in BOHEMIA_MUSIC_REPO.txt: every voice, its owner song, its batch.
