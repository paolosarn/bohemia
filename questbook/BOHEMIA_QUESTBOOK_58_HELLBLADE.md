# BOHEMIA QUESTBOOK — DEEP DIVE 58: MENTAL ILLNESS FROM THE INSIDE / DUTY-OF-CARE HORROR (Hellblade: Senua's Sacrifice)
Full teardown, the whole enchilada: portraying psychosis FROM THE INSIDE, binaural 3D audio for auditory
hallucinations, the mental-health-community + Wellcome Trust collaboration, the voices-as-characters, the
permadeath BLUFF, the visual-hallucination camera tricks, the support-line duty-of-care, the honest
critiques (psychosis-as-superpower/plot-device), and Bohemia ports. This is the medium's model for
RESPONSIBLE, RESEARCHED, EMBODIED mental-illness horror — the DUTY-OF-CARE counterpoint to DDLC's (Q57)
shock-value misstep. DIRECTLY relevant to handling the Amalgamation's psychological horror responsibly.
Ninja Theory (Tameem Antoniades). Reference only; Paolo does not read it. No Bohemia quest written here.

CONTENT NOTE: Hellblade centers on psychosis, trauma, grief, and (in its themes) suicide/loss. This
teardown analyzes the CRAFT + the studio's DUTY-OF-CARE. Any Bohemia port MUST follow our wellbeing/tone/
child-safety rules (see PORTS).

Game: Hellblade: Senua's Sacrifice (Ninja Theory, 2017). A dark action-adventure. Senua, a Pict warrior
with psychosis ("the darkness"), journeys into a mythic Norse underworld (Hel) to save her dead lover's
soul, blaming herself for his death + her village's massacre. Built in close consultation with
psychologists + people with lived experience of psychosis. 5 BAFTAs; a landmark of mental-illness
representation + audio design.

===============================================================================
## 0. THE CORE IDEA (why it's in the canon)
===============================================================================
- MENTAL ILLNESS FROM THE INSIDE (embodied, not observed): most media depict mental illness from the
  OUTSIDE (a symptom others watch). Hellblade puts the PLAYER INSIDE Senua's psychosis — you HEAR her
  voices, SEE her hallucinations, share her unreliable perception. It's "a profound, embodied exploration
  of mental disability, set apart from media that depict psychosis from a purely observational standpoint."
  The player EXPERIENCES the condition, building empathy through embodiment (cf. Disco's internal voices
  Q05, SH2 subjective-world Q35; first-person subjectivity as empathy).
- RESEARCHED DUTY-OF-CARE (the responsible-treatment model — the KEY contrast to DDLC Q57): the studio
  collaborated closely with psychologists, neuroscientist Dr Charles Fernyhough, the Wellcome Trust, and a
  support group (The Voice Collective) of people with LIVED EXPERIENCE — to depict psychosis "in a
  respectful, non-stigmatizing fashion." The game opens with mental-health context + links support lines,
  and states psychosis is "real, common, and treatable." This is how to handle dark mental-health content
  RESPONSIBLY — the exact duty DDLC (Q57) was criticized for shirking (cf. our wellbeing/tone rules;
  THE model for responsible depiction).
- AUDIO AS THE PRIMARY EXPERIENTIAL TOOL (binaural voices — the signature craft): the defining feature is
  the CHORUS OF VOICES in Senua's mind, recorded with BINAURAL 3D microphones so they move around your
  head — directional auditory hallucinations that whisper, direct, reward, punish, doubt, and comfort.
  Sound (heard through headphones) is the main vehicle of the psychosis experience — validated by
  Fernyhough as authentic to described symptoms (cf. our music/audio strengths; audio as an empathy +
  horror engine).

## 1. THE ARCHITECTURE (how psychosis is made playable)
===============================================================================

### THE VOICES AS CHARACTERS (a chorus, not noise)
- The "Furies" are a persistent, MULTI-VOICED presence: some mock + undermine ("you'll fail"), some warn
  of enemies behind you (a gameplay function), some comfort, some narrate. They speak about Senua in the
  third person AND to the player — blurring who they address. They're an information source, a threat, and
  a characterization device at once — the inner experience externalized as a cast (cf. Disco skills-as-
  voices Q05, GLaDOS Q36; the voice-chorus as mechanic + character + theme).

### VISUAL HALLUCINATION + THE "FOCUS" MECHANIC (perception as gameplay)
- High-fidelity facial capture (Melina Juergens) + environmental camera tricks replicate visual
  irregularities reported by people with psychosis — faces in rock, shifting geometry, hidden patterns.
  The core PUZZLE mechanic is "focus": Senua perceives runes/alignments in the environment that only
  resolve from the right perspective — apophenia (seeing patterns/meaning others don't) turned into a
  MECHANIC. Her condition IS the gameplay lens (cf. Obra Dinn perception Q19, our perception/[READ]; the
  symptom as a verb).

### THE PERMADEATH BLUFF (fear conveyed directly — the clever/controversial trick)
- Early on, Senua's hand "rots"; the Darkness says each DEATH spreads the rot, and if it reaches her head,
  it's PERMADEATH — your SAVE will be deleted + all progress erased. This created real player dread of
  losing everything. It was a BLUFF — the rot never actually reaches her head; the wording was chosen to
  transmit the FEAR associated with the condition directly to the player. A psychological trick on the
  PLAYER, mirroring Senua's dread (cf. our death-stakes/tension design; but note the honesty debate below).

### TRAUMA + GRIEF AS THE ROOT (the human core under the myth)
- The psychosis is entwined with TRAUMA + GRIEF: her lover's death, her village's massacre, self-blame,
  a cruel father, religious stigma. The Norse-underworld journey is a MYTHIC EXTERNALIZATION of grief +
  guilt (like SH2's town Q35). Meeting Druth (another sufferer) eases her belief she's "cursed/tainted" —
  connection as relief. The human wound under the fantasy is the real subject (cf. SH2 Q35, our grief/
  guilt spine).

### FOCUSED, SHORT, INTIMATE SCOPE (a small team's strength)
- ~8 hours, linear, one character, no HUD — an "independent AAA" made affordably by a small team, sold
  cheap, betting that ATMOSPHERE + performance + audio + a single focused idea beat scope. Intimacy +
  focus over open-world sprawl (cf. our single-district focus, Chrono compact-but-huge Q50; depth over size).

## 2. THE HONEST FLAWS (banked)
===============================================================================
- "PSYCHOSIS AS SUPERPOWER" / PLOT-DEVICE CRITIQUE (the central caution): Edge deplored that Senua's
  psychosis reads as "something of an ASSET: a helpful superpower... so long as you put up with the odd
  breakdown," and Mic's Faulkner (who worked with the mentally ill) argued it uses the condition as a PLOT
  DEVICE rather than making genuine understanding the focus. LESSON (crucial for us): even researched,
  well-meant depiction can slip into romanticizing/instrumentalizing illness (making it a power or a plot
  engine). Handle with care: the condition is a HUMAN REALITY, not a gameplay gimmick or a superpower
  (our wellbeing/tone rules; the line even a "good" example can cross).
- ILLNESS CONFLATED WITH ROT/INFECTION/CURSE: the game literally ties psychosis to PUTREFACTION (the
  rotting arm) + curse/taint imagery — which academics flag as risking a stigmatizing "disease" framing.
  LESSON: metaphors for mental illness (rot, darkness, corruption) can STIGMATIZE if not handled carefully;
  avoid equating a real condition with contamination/evil (our tone/wellbeing; a specific DON'T for our
  glitch/corruption Amalgamation imagery — keep it about the AI, not a person's mind).
- THE PERMADEATH BLUFF WAS ARGUABLY DISHONEST: some felt lied to (the threatened save-deletion never
  happens) — a manipulation that risks player trust. LESSON: psychological tricks on the player can
  backfire into feeling cheated; weigh impact vs trust (cf. our fairness/honesty; don't threaten
  consequences we won't honor).
- REPRESENTATION IS INHERENTLY CONTESTED: responses ranged from deeply moved (players who saw their
  experience honored) to critical (sufferers who felt misrepresented) — no single depiction satisfies all.
  LESSON: mental-illness representation is high-stakes + will be scrutinized; consult widely, stay humble,
  and don't claim to speak for everyone (our humility + consultation ethos).
- DRAMATIC LICENSE VS ACCURACY TENSION: it blends real symptoms with Norse myth + fantasy — powerful, but
  the fantastical framing can muddy the "real." LESSON: if we use a real condition, keep the human truth
  legible under the fantasy (our authored spine over metaphor).

## 4. BOHEMIA PORTS (directions, not built) — the RESPONSIBLE-depiction model for our psychological horror
===============================================================================
Hellblade is the DUTY-OF-CARE model for dark mental-health/psychological content — the responsible
counterpoint to DDLC (Q57). Its audio-as-empathy + voices-as-characters + embodied-subjectivity are
directly useful for our Amalgamation horror + our audio strengths; its FLAWS mark the exact lines our
wellbeing/tone rules must hold.
- W2 (researched duty-of-care — the ABSOLUTE model): bank Hellblade as HOW to handle any dark mental-health/
  trauma content in Bohemia RESPONSIBLY — if we touch grief, trauma, or psychological breakdown (our
  conscience/mental-health system Q41, the collapse's human toll), we do it with CARE: research/consultation
  where possible, context + support resources, a "real, common, treatable" framing, and NEVER as shock or
  gimmick. This is the DDLC-contrast lesson made concrete — our wellbeing/tone/child-safety rules are the
  spine (ties our conscience system Q41, Darkest Dungeon stress Q41, This War of Mine Q30; the responsible line).
- W1/W3/W4 (embodied subjectivity + binaural audio + voices-as-characters — our AUDIO strength + the
  Amalgamation): bank AUDIO as a primary empathy + horror engine — Bohemia can put the player INSIDE a
  subjective experience via sound (the Amalgamation's whispers, the voices of the uploaded dead, a chorus
  that mocks/warns/comforts). This plays STRAIGHT to our music/audio strengths + is deeply on-theme: the
  Amalgamation IS a chorus of millions of uploaded voices (ties SOMA Q34, GLaDOS Q36, Disco voices Q05,
  our music repo + Amalgamation-voice + New Voices Law; headphone-first binaural voices = a signature).
- W5 (perception as gameplay — the symptom/condition as a verb): consider making a subjective PERCEPTION a
  MECHANIC (the dynasty/a character perceives patterns/truths others can't — the [READ], seeing the
  Amalgamation's edits, apophenia in the ledger) — the lens IS the gameplay (ties Obra Dinn Q19, Golden
  Idol Q54, our [READ]/perception; but keep it about the AI/mystery, not stigmatizing a person's mind — see
  flaws).
- W6 (the death-stakes trick — with the HONESTY caution): our roguelite/fold death-stakes can transmit
  real dread — but bank the FLAW: don't threaten consequences we won't honor (the permadeath-bluff felt
  dishonest to some); our fairness/honesty + regression-gate mean stakes are REAL, not bluffed (ties our
  fold/roguelite, the fairness principle).
- W7 (trauma/grief as the root — the human core under the myth): our grief/guilt spine + the collapse's
  human toll should sit UNDER the Amalgamation myth like Senua's wound under the Norse journey — the
  Amalgamation horror lands because it's about real GRIEF (the uploaded dead, lost loved ones, self-blame)
  (ties SH2 Q35, SOMA Q34, our grief/guilt spine + dynasty/family stakes Q47; keep the human truth legible).
- W8/W9/W10 (focused intimate scope + no-HUD + atmosphere/performance): Hellblade VALIDATES our single-
  district focus + small-scale ambition — atmosphere + audio + one focused idea beats scope; consider
  minimal-HUD immersion where legibility allows (ties Chrono compact Q50, Gothic/Qud atmosphere Q51/Q56,
  our single-district + art/music strengths + solo scale; "independent AAA" is our lane).
- THE FLAW-LINES (bank HARDEST — our wellbeing/tone guardrails): (a) NEVER frame a real condition as a
  SUPERPOWER or a mere PLOT-DEVICE (the Edge/Mic critique) — mental-health reality is not a gameplay
  gimmick; (b) do NOT equate a real condition with ROT/INFECTION/CURSE/evil (the stigmatizing-metaphor
  risk) — our Amalgamation glitch/corruption imagery must be about the AI/the edited data, NOT a person's
  mind; (c) don't threaten consequences we won't honor (the bluff-honesty line); (d) stay HUMBLE + consult —
  representation is contested + scrutinized, no depiction satisfies all; (e) keep the human truth legible
  under any myth/metaphor. These are our get-it-right lines (ties our wellbeing/tone/child-safety rules).
