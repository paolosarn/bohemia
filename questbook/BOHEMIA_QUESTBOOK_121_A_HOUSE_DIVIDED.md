# BOHEMIA QUESTBOOK — DEEP DIVE 121: KILL THEM OR CHANGE THEIR MINDS, AND THE MACHINE WON'T TELL YOU WHICH IS MERCY (Legion: A House Divided, Mass Effect 2)
Full teardown, the whole enchilada: the loyalty mission where an AI asks you to help destroy the members of
its own species who chose differently, then reveals halfway through that it could instead **rewrite their
minds**, then refuses to have an opinion, then defers the decision to you **because you're the one who shot
them.** Complete event flow, the alternative, the abstention, the EMP, the argument, the honest flaws, and
Bohemia ports. STARTLINGLY relevant to our AMALGAMATION, our NETWORK, our conscience system, our faction
web, our companions, + our Liberate/Respect/Become. BioWare. Reference only; Paolo does not read it. No
Bohemia quest written here.

Quest: "Legion: A House Divided," Mass Effect 2 (2010). "**THE GETH HERETICS — GETH THAT WORSHIP THE REAPERS
AND THE ONES SHEPARD FOUGHT IN THE FIRST GAME — ARE PLANNING TO INTRODUCE A VIRUS INTO THE GETH THAT WILL
FORC[E]**" them all to convert. You go to stop it.

## 1. THE FULL EVENT FLOW (stage by stage)
===============================================================================

### STAGE 0 — YOU HAD TO NOT SELL HIM FIRST
- "**IT'S IMPORTANT TO NOTE THAT THIS DECISION IS ONLY OPEN TO YOU IF YOU HAVE LEGION AS A PARTY MEMBER,
  **MEANING YOU CAN'T SELL THE GETH TO CERBERUS.**"
- **The entire mission is gated behind not selling a person.** Hours earlier, someone offered to buy him, and
  if you took the money this quest doesn't exist (STARTLINGLY our conscience system + persistent consequence
  + standing + companions; the content you lose by trading a person).
### STAGE 1 — THE CLOCK IS INVISIBLE AND ALREADY RUNNING
- "**RECOVERING LEGION FROM THE REAPER IFF **STARTS AN INVISIBLE TIMER TOWARDS THE END OF THE GAME'S PLOT,
  THE SUICIDE MISSION.** PLAYERS WILL ONLY HAVE A SMALL WINDOW OF **1-3 QUESTS** BEFORE THE COLLECTORS AMBUSH
  THE NORMANDY AND KIDNAP THE CREW.**"
- And the resulting triage, laid out by a guide as a genuine dilemma: "**TRIGGER A HOUSE DIVIDED RIGHT AWAY:
  USE WHAT LITTLE TIME LEFT TO HAVE A LOYAL LEGION HELP SAVE THE CREW · **CONDUCT THE SUICIDE MISSION WITH A
  DISLOYAL LEGION**: TAKE LEGION ON THE FINAL MISSION, THEN COMPLETE A HOUSE DIVIDED IF HE SURVIVES.**"
- **The last squadmate you recruit arrives with a countdown attached**, and doing his personal quest costs
  you your crew (cf. our scheduler + survival accounting + conscience system + Megaton law).
### STAGE 2 — THE STATION HAS NO WINDOWS AND NO AIR
- "**JOKER REMARKS THAT SHOULD ANY GETH LOOK OUT A WINDOW THEY ARE NOT CLOAKED TO THE VISIBLE SPECTRUM;
  HOWEVER, **LEGION REPLIES THAT WINDOWS ARE A STRUCTURAL WEAKNESS THAT ARE NOT PRESENT ON THE STATION.**"
- "**WHEN YOU ARRIVE LEGION TELLS SHEPARD THAT **THERE IS NO AIR AND LITTLE GRAVITY BECAUSE THE GETH REQUIRE
  NEITHER.**"
- **The architecture is a personality.** No windows because windows are a flaw. No air because nobody
  breathes. You are trespassing in a building designed by people who don't have your needs (STARTLINGLY our
  district + HD tile repo + prefab factory + AMALGAMATION + city-builder; the level as a species'
  worldview).
### STAGE 3 — THE ENEMIES ARE ASLEEP AT THEIR DESKS
- "**NOT ALL GETH WILL BE ACTIVE ON THE SHIP, MOSTLY APPLYING TO ROOMS WITH GLOWING GREEN TECH PATHWAYS.
  AVOID STEPPING ON THESE AS THEY WILL SOUND ALARMS AND ACTIVE GETH FROM NEARBY NODES... **WHILE GETH ARE AT
  THESE NODES, THEY'RE VULNERABLE TO ATTACKS AND WILL NOT PAY ATTENTION TO SHEPARD OR THE SQUAD, GIVING YOU
  AN OPENING.**"
- And: "**SHOOTING THE NODES WILL CAUSE THEM TO EXPLODE AND DO SERIOUS DAMAGE TO ENEMIES ON THEM. ONCE
  THEY'RE DEALT WITH, **THESE NODES CAN ALSO BE SALVAGED FOR CREDITS.**"

  **Then you loot the rack for money** (STARTLINGLY our PACIFIST LAW + combat dial + conscience system +

### STAGE 4 — THE CONVERSATION IS THE MISSION AND IT'S SCORED
- "**ONCE THIS FIRST ONE IS DESTROYED, A CONVERSATION WITH LEGION WILL TAKE PLACE, GIVING YOU MORE
  INFORMATION ABOUT THE GETH. **SAYING THE HERETICS ARE STILL GETH WILL EARN +2 PARAGON, AS WILL SAYING THAT
  REWRITING THEM COULD BE TRAUMATIZING. SAYING LEGION FEELS GUILTY WILL EARN +2 RENEGADE.**"
- **"Saying that rewriting them could be traumatizing" is worth Paragon points.** The game knows the mercy

### STAGE 5 — THE ROOM WHERE YOU DECIDE

  HERETICS.**"
- "**YOU CAN, FOR THE LAST TIME, **ASK FOR LEGION'S OPINION AND AGAIN IT CANNOT DECIDE.**"

## 2. THE HONEST FLAWS (banked)
===============================================================================
- THE CHOICE IS COSMETIC IN ITS OWN GAME: "**IN MASS EFFECT 2, THOSE ARE THE ONLY REAL CONSEQUENCES FOR YOUR
  DECISION - BESIDES THE OCCASIONAL BIT OF DIALOGUE.**" An EMP flash or an explosion. LESSON (~2nd
  confirmation with Q119): **a consequence that only pays out in a sequel is a promissory note** — our fold
  has three dynasties, so we can actually cash it, but each generation must feel the bill.
- IT'S BOLTED TO A MORALITY METER, WHICH SETTLES THE QUESTION FOR YOU: "**REWRITING THE GETH FOR A HUGE +30
  PARAGON OR DESTROY THE STATION FOR ANOTHER BIG +30 RENEGADE.**" And a guide, plainly: "**THE MOST BASIC
  THING THAT A PLAYER SHOULD CONSIDER... IS **IF THEY HAVE BEEN SLANTING THEIR PLAYTHROUGH TOWARD PARAGON OR
  RENEGADE.**" LESSON (ours, the reason we have NO KARMA BAR): the second you put +30 PARAGON on one button,
  **the moral dilemma becomes a build decision.** Players don't ask "what's right," they ask "what am I
  playing."
- LEGION IS LOYAL EITHER WAY: "**REGARDLESS OF WHAT ACTION YOU TAKE AT THIS POINT IN THE MISSION AS FAR AS
  REWRITING OR KILLING THE GETH GOES, **LEGION WILL BE HAPPY WITH THE OUTCOME AND WILL BECOME LOYAL.**"
  LESSON (double-edged): an abstention this total means the relationship isn't at stake — our standing should
  let someone be **quietly disappointed** and still stay.
- THE COMBAT IS SKIPPABLE AND THE GUIDE SAYS SO: "**TIP: YOU DO NOT HAVE TO DEFEAT ALL THE ENEMIES TO
  COMPLETE THE MISSION – **TAPPING THE AIRLOCK AT THE OTHER END OF THE ROOM WILL RESULT IN INSTANT VICTORY
  AND A CUTSCENE.**" LESSON: if your climax can be finished by touching a door, the fight was set dressing.
- THE PEACE MATH INVERTS THE MORALITY: destroying them "**PUTS TWO POINTS TOWARD UNLOCKING THE OPTION TO
  ESTABLISH PEACE.**" The Renegade genocide is the pro-peace vote. LESSON (double-edged, and it's mostly a
  triumph): consequence that contradicts the label is great writing AND players who read a wiki first will
  optimize the ledger and never feel the dilemma.
- THE OPTIMAL PLAY IS TO NOT DO THE QUEST YET: "**CONDUCT THE SUICIDE MISSION WITH A DISLOYAL LEGION.**"
  LESSON (~3rd confirmation): if your timer makes players postpone character content, the timer is fighting
  the writing.
