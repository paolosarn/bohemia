# BOHEMIA QUESTBOOK — DEEP DIVE 112: HE FELT MORE DIGNIFIED WHILE HUNG FROM THE TREE / THE SMELL IS A SKILL CHECK (The Hanged Man, Disco Elysium)
Full teardown, the whole enchilada: the murder investigation where the first obstacle is that you cannot get
close enough to the body to look at it, the second is that nobody will help you cut it down unless you
adopt their politics, and the third is that the entire crime scene is a lie that the corpse itself is
keeping. Complete event flow, the Endurance check, Cuno's rocks, Measurehead, the autopsy form, the bullet,
the boots, the honest flaws, and Bohemia ports. STARTLINGLY relevant to our [READ], our death-math, our
conscience system, our standing, our faction web, our MEDICINE currency, + our ART PHILOSOPHY. ZA/UM.
Reference only; Paolo does not read it. No Bohemia quest written here.

Quest: "Inspect victim's body" → "Getting the body down" → "Perform field autopsy" → "Find the bullet before
you get fooled," Disco Elysium (2019). "**THE HANGED MAN IS AT THE CENTER OF EVERYTHING IN DISCO ELYSIUM...
HE SERVES AS A REMINDER THAT AS A DETECTIVE, YOU SHOULD PROBABLY DEAL WITH THIS WHOLE MURDER THING.**"

## 0. THE CORE IDEA (why it's in the canon)
===============================================================================
- THE FIRST BOSS IS A SMELL (the premise — STARTLINGLY our death-math + survival accounting): "**WHEN HARRY
  APPROACHES THE DECOMPOSING BODY, HE GETS AN OBJECT LESSON IN WHY DEALING WITH CORPSES IS UNPLEASANT,
  ESPECIALLY WHEN THEY'VE BEEN HANGING IN A TREE FOR A WEEK. TO ACTUALLY GET CLOSE ENOUGH TO INSPECT IT, HE
  NEED[S] TO DEAL WITH THE SMELL OF AN UNGODLY ROT. **HE'LL NEED TO PASS A LEGENDARY ENDURANCE CHECK (14 IN
  TOTAL).**"
  - **The obstacle between you and the murder victim is your own gag reflex.** Not a lock. Not a guard. The
    physical reality of a week-old body in a tree, expressed as the hardest stat check in the opening hours
    (STARTLINGLY our death-math + district + [READ]; the corpse that defends itself with decomposition).
- AND EVERY SOLUTION COSTS YOU SOMETHING UGLY (the design — direct for our conscience system): there is no
  clean way to get him down. Shoot the clasp with a borrowed police pistol. Or **subscribe to a racist's
  ideology.** Or go beg a corrupt union boss who will want a favor forever.
  - "**THERE ARE TWO WAYS TO GET MEASUREHEAD TO HELP YOU REMOVE THE BODY FROM THE TREE. **YOU CAN EITHER
    SUBSCRIBE TO HIS EXTREMELY PROBLEMATIC IDEOLOGY OR GET HIS BOSS TO MAKE HIM HELP YOU.**"
  - "**DOING THIS WILL MAKE KIM THINK LESS OF YOU THOUGH**" (STARTLINGLY our conscience system + standing +
    faction web; the favor with an ideological price tag).
- AND THE BODY IS LYING TO YOU (the twist — STARTLINGLY our [READ] + recorded-vs-unrecorded): "**IT TURNS OUT
  THAT THE HANGING WAS MERELY MISDIRECTION, AND THE VICTIM HAD BEEN SHOT THROUGH THE MOUTH, THE BULLET STILL
  LODGED INSIDE HIS SKULL.**"
  - The crime scene is a **staging.** The rope, the tree, the whole tableau you spent two days fighting to
    reach — it's a prop. The real evidence is inside his head, and the game hides it behind "**A LEGENDARY
    PERCEPTION CHECK**" that most players fail (cf. our [READ] + recorded-vs-unrecorded; the scene that is a
    forgery).

## 1. THE FULL EVENT FLOW (stage by stage)

### STAGE 0 — A CHILD IS THROWING ROCKS AT IT
- "**THE HANGED MAN CAN BE FOUND JUST BEHIND THE WHIRLING-IN-RAGS. WHEN YOU LEAVE THROUGH THE DOORS, WALK UP
  AND THEN HEAD THROUGH THE BROKEN FENCE. IN THE YARD, YOU'LL FIND **CUNO RESPECTFULLY THROWING STONES OFF
  THE CADAVER.**"
- That's the establishing shot. A murder victim has been hanging in a public yard for a week and **a child is
  using him for target practice**, and nobody has done anything, because nobody is coming. That's Martinaise
  in one image (STARTLINGLY our district + entities + city-builder + ART PHILOSOPHY; neglect as scenery).
### STAGE 1 — YOU THROW UP (and the game is kind about it)
- "**AT THIS STAGE OF THE INVESTIGATION, HARRIER WILL PROBABLY VOMIT, AND **KIM WILL REACT VERY INDULGENTLY -
  HE WILL SAY THAT IT HAPPENS TO EVERYONE** AND THAT AMMONIA CAN HELP OFFSET THE BAD SMELL OF DECOMPOSING
  FLESH - YOU CAN GET THIS CHEMICAL FROM THE GARDENER.**"
- Your partner watches you vomit at a crime scene on day one and says **it happens to everyone.** No penalty.
  No contempt. He just tells you where to get ammonia.
- And the fix is a **neighbor**: "**APPROACH THE WOMAN SITTING NEAR THE WHIRLING-IN-RAG HOTEL AND ASK HER FOR
  AMMONIA... IF YOU WANT, YOU CAN ALSO ASK FOR HER GLOVES – SHE WILL GLADLY GIVE THEM TO YOU.**" A gardener
  hands you rubber gloves for a murder (cf. our companions + MEDICINE currency + district).
- The other bonuses are pure Disco: "**FAIL THE ENDURANCE CHECK TWICE TO GET THE 'VOLUMETRIC SHIT COMPRESSOR'
  THOUGHT. INTERNALIZE IT FOR +6 FOR THE CHECK... TALK TO CUNO AFTER THROWING UP AND COIN THE 'CUNOIFIED
  NONVOMITOR' (+1).**"
- **Failing twice is a progression path.** The game rewards you for being bad at this in a specific way
  (STARTLINGLY our conscience system + quest factory + difficulty packages; failure as a build).
### STAGE 2 — THE PRELIMINARY (reading the body from six feet away)
- "**ONCE HARRY HAS GOTTEN HIMSELF TOGETHER, HE WILL DO A PRELIMINARY INSPECTION OF THE VICTIM. HE'LL BE
  WORKING WITH KIM TO GET THE VARIOUS FEATURES OF THE BODY.**"
- The belt: "**PASSING A MEDIUM PASSIVE ELECTROCHEMISTRY CHECK WILL REVEAL THAT HE WAS HANGED WITH A
  **VERMILLION REINFORCED CARGO LASHING BELT USED FOR AIRSHIP TRANSPORTATION.** YOU CAN REVEAL THAT TO KIM,
  AND HE WILL POINT OUT THAT SIX-ROTORS ARE USED FOR SHUFFLING CONTAINERS AT THE DOCKS.**"
- **The murder weapon is industrial equipment**, which means the murderers are dock workers, which means the
  union, which means the strike. The rope tells you the politics (cf. our [READ] + faction web + logistics).
- And the impossible one: "**LOOKING INTO HIS EYE ALLOWS FOR [AN] ATTEMPT [AT] A MEDIUM INLAND EMPIRE CHECK.
  SUCCEEDING WILL BEGIN A CONVERSATION WITH THE DEAD MAN IN WHICH YOU CAN LEARN MORE ABOUT HIM AND HOW HE
  DIED.**"
- **You can interview the corpse.** It's a skill check. Your own subconscious plays the part of a dead
  mercenary, and what it tells you is real information (STARTLINGLY our [READ] + AMALGAMATION + death-math;
  the dead man who talks because you need him to).
### STAGE 3 — GETTING HIM DOWN (three doors, all of them cost)
- **DOOR 1 — the gun**: "**THE FASTEST AND LEAST PROFESSIONAL ROUTE TO REMOVING THE BODY FROM THE TREE IS TO
  USE YOUR FIREARM TO DESTROY THE CLASP HOLDING THE NOOSE IN PLACE. UNFORTUNATELY, YOU PROBABLY DON'T HAVE
  YOUR GUN YET. **BUT THANKFULLY, KIM DOES.**"

  - "**IF YOU'RE SUCCESSFUL, THE CLASP WILL SHATTER AND DROP THE BODY TO THE FLOOR... MORE IMPORTANTLY, IT

    And "**DOING THIS WILL MAKE KIM THINK LESS OF YOU**" (STARTLINGLY our conscience system + standing;

- **DOOR 3 — the union**: go ask Evrart Claire. "**YOUR FIRST CONVERSATION WITH THE UNION BOSS WILL BE
  UNPLEASANT, AND HARRIER WILL LOSE 1 HEALTH POINT IF HE SITS IN A CHAIR.**" **The chair damages you.**
  - "**EVRART CLAIRE WON'T WANT TO GET INVOLVED AT FIRST, BUT KIM WILL JOIN THE CONVERSATION AND SAY THAT THE

### STAGE 4 — THE AUTOPSY IS A FORM (the greatest paperwork scene in games)
- "**THIS WILL NEED A FORM TO BE FILLED, **IN FOUR PARTS.**" And: "**SHOW KIM THE RED FIELD AUTOPSY FORM IN
  YOUR LEDGER (+5 XP). KIM WILL ASSIGN A CASE NUMBER 'KK57...'. SUGGEST THAT YOU FILE THE CORONER'S CASE
  UNDER YOUR ID.**"
- "**PASS A DRAMA 10 CHECK TO REMEMBER THE CORRECT ALPHANUMERIC.**" **You roll dice to remember your own

- The structure: "**AT THE START, KIM ASKS ABOUT THE CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM... THE INTERNAL EXAMINATION
  FOCUSES ON THE MOUTH AND HYOID BONE, WHICH KIM INVESTIGATES ON HIS OWN. **THE REST OF THE REPORT, INCLUDING
  HEPATOBILIARY ANALYSIS, TOXICOLOGY, SEROLOGY, IS MARKED AS N/A.**"
- **Most of the form is marked N/A**, because you're two cops in a yard with chaincutters and no lab. The

- And the tone never breaks either way: "**YOU CAN ELECT TO TOUCH THE DEAD MAN'S PENIS UPON INSPECTION, MUCH

- **Clean hands.** A man who was lynched by a mob has no defensive wounds and clean fingernails. That detail

  important clue in the game is inside the mouth, and looking there damages you.**

## 2. THE HONEST FLAWS (banked)
===============================================================================
- THE CENTRAL CLUE IS BEHIND A CHECK MOST PLAYERS FAIL: the bullet needs a "**LEGENDARY 14 PERCEPTION WHITE
  CHECK.**" Miss it and you complete the entire murder investigation **without ever learning how he died**,
  and the game lets you. LESSON (ours, double-edged): gating your thesis behind a dice roll is honest and it
  means a chunk of your audience never sees the game you wrote — our [READ] needs a FLOOR (the minimum truth
  everyone gets) as well as a ceiling.
- THE FRIDGE IS A SAVE-SCUM WITH EXTRA STEPS: "**FRIDGING IT GIVES +2 TO THE PERCEPTION CHECK.**" The
  emotional weight of failing an autopsy is neutralized by a bear freezer and a stat buff. LESSON: if a
  failure state has a mechanical undo, it's a difficulty setting wearing a story's clothes.
- THE RACIST IS THE EASY BUTTON: internalizing **Advanced Race Theory** is a real, effective, permanent
  build path with stat bonuses. Kim thinking less of you is the only cost. LESSON (ours, careful): if the
  ugly ideology is also the optimal build, some players will take it for the numbers and learn nothing —
  our conscience system must not make a worldview into a stat stick without a bill.
- THE BODY IS A CONTENT LOCK ON YOUR PARTNER: "**BAGGING HIM DISPATCHES THE BODY AWAY AND **REMOVES KIM FOR
  THE DAY.**" Finishing the autopsy correctly deletes your best character from the game for a day. LESSON:
  don't punish completion by removing the thing players like most.
- ORDER-OF-OPERATIONS TRAPS EVERYWHERE: "**BEFORE YOU PROCEED WITH THIS STEP, MAKE SURE YOU'VE FOUND THE DEAD
  MAN'S BOOTS... AND MADE THE ORB APPEAR - IF YOU DECIDE TO DO IT LATER, KIM WILL BE AWAY UNTIL THE END OF
  THE DAY.**" Guides exist because the sequencing is unforgiving and unsignposted. LESSON (~6th
  confirmation): silent sequencing requirements are bugs in design costumes.
- THE DICK JOKES SIT ON TOP OF THE BEST WRITING: "**YOU CAN ELECT TO TOUCH THE DEAD MAN'S PENIS.**" It's
  tonally deliberate and it is also the thing that makes a portion of players quit before the good part.
  LESSON: your tone is a filter — know who it filters out.
