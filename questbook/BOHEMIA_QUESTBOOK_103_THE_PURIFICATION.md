# BOHEMIA QUESTBOOK — DEEP DIVE 103: SHE HANDS YOU YOUR ORDERS TO KILL HER / THE FAMILY WAS INNOCENT (The Purification, Oblivion)
Full teardown, the whole enchilada: the quest where the guild that recruited you, trained you, gave you a
home and called you family orders you to murder every single one of them on suspicion of a traitor — and
the traitor was never in the building. Complete event flow, the sealed letter Ocheeva hands you herself,
the seven names, the poisoned apples, the promotion, the reveal that it was all for nothing, the honest
flaws, and Bohemia ports. STARTLINGLY relevant to our FACTION WEB, our standing, our conscience system, our
AMALGAMATION, + our fold. Bethesda. Reference only; Paolo does not read it. No Bohemia quest written here.

Quest: "The Purification," Dark Brotherhood questline, The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion (2006). You've spent
the whole questline being welcomed into the Cheydinhal Sanctuary: contracts from Vicente, then Ocheeva, a
bed, a key, a necklace, colleagues. Then Lucien Lachance, a Speaker of the Black Hand, gives you a list.
The list is everyone.

## 0. THE CORE IDEA (why it's in the canon)
===============================================================================
- THE VICTIM HANDS YOU THE ORDER TO KILL HER, SEALED, UNREAD (the opening — STARTLINGLY our faction web +
  standing): "**FOR THIS QUEST, OCHEEVA SIMPLY HANDS YOU A LETTER FROM LUCIEN LACHANCE AND COMMENTS ON HOW
  SHE KNOWS THIS IS AN ASSIGNMENT THAT COMES DIRECTLY FROM THE BLACK HAND, AND AS SUCH, SHE DOESN'T KNOW ITS
  CONTENTS.**"
  - Your boss, who likes you, passes you an envelope she is not permitted to open. Inside is her own death
    warrant and the death warrants of everyone she loves. She hands it over cheerfully because the chain of
    command is the chain of command.
  - "**THE LETTER ALSO WARNS YOU THAT YOU MUST NOT SPEAK TO ANYONE IN THE SANCTUARY ABOUT THE LETTER**" (cf.
    our faction web + standing + conscience system; the institution using its own loyalty as the murder
    weapon).
- YOU ARE CHOSEN BECAUSE YOU'RE NEW (the logic, and it's chillingly sound): "**LUCIEN'S SILENCER, THE HERO,
  WAS RECRUITED AFTER THE SPY WAS KNOWN TO THE BLACK HAND AND IS THEREFORE ABOVE SUSPICION, SO THEY ARE
  CHARGED WITH THE TASK.**"
  - "**THE TREACHEROUS ACTIVITIES STARTED BEFORE YOU JOINED THE BROTHERHOOD, WHICH CLEARS YOU OF
    SUSPICION.**"
  - The only reason you're trusted with this is that you haven't been there long enough to love them. Your
    shallow attachment is your qualification (STARTLINGLY our standing + fold; recency as clearance).
- AND THE TRAITOR WAS NEVER IN THAT BUILDING (the reveal that recontextualizes everything): "**THE TRAITOR
  WAS A SPEAKER NAMED MATHIEU BELLAMONT, WHO SOUGHT VENGEANCE FOR THE BROTHERHOOD'S KILLING OF HIS MOTHER
  MANY YEARS PRIOR. UNAWARE OF THIS, LACHANCE ORDERED THE PURIFICATION OF THE ENTIRE CHEYDINHAL
  SANCTUARY.**"
  - Bellamont was a **SPEAKER.** Higher rank than the sanctuary. Not on the list. Not in the room. He was
    upstairs the whole time.
  - "**BELLAMONT'S BETRAYAL CONTINUED AND ULTIMATELY RESULTED IN THE ELIMINATION OF HALF THE BLACK HAND,
    INCLUDING LACHANCE HIMSELF.**"
  - You murdered seven innocent people, on orders, to catch a spy who wasn't among them — and it didn't even
    slow him down. Then he killed the man who gave you the order (STARTLINGLY our AMALGAMATION + faction web
    + Megaton law; the atrocity that accomplished nothing).

## 1. THE FULL EVENT FLOW (stage by stage)
===============================================================================

### STAGE 0 — THE BRIDGE QUEST IS CALLED "OF SECRET AND SHADOW"
- "**THE 'OF SECRET AND SHADOW' MINI-QUEST DIRECTS YOU TO TALK TO OCHEEVA. SHE TELLS YOU THAT YOU HAVE
  RECEIVED A LETTER FROM LUCIEN. APPARENTLY THE BLACK HAND ITSELF HAS A TASK FOR YOU.**"
- She's PLEASED for you. The Black Hand noticed her recruit. That's a promotion in her house (cf. our
  standing + faction web).
### STAGE 1 — THE FORT (and the tree)
- "**YOU ARE INSTRUCTED TO GO TO FORT FARRAGUT AND FIGHT YOUR WAY THROUGH THE UNDEAD SENTINELS UNTIL YOU
  REACH LUCIEN.**" "**THE TOWER IS FULL OF UNDEAD AND TRAPS.**"
- Or: "**THERE IS A HIDDEN EXIT FROM LUCIEN'S ROOM TO A TRAP DOOR IN A TREE TO THE NORTHWEST OF THE FORT.**"
  "**THIS DOOR IS LOCATED JUST OUTSIDE FORT FARRAGUT, INSIDE A HOLLOWED OUT TREE.**"
- The man who runs the region's assassins lives behind a dungeon and keeps a **hollow tree** as his back
  door. It's the best-hidden entrance in the game and it's just... a tree (cf. our district + prefab
  factory + HD tile repo).
### STAGE 2 — THE LIST (seven names, read aloud)
- Lucien's line, verbatim, and it's the whole quest: "**OCHEEVA, VICENTE VALTIERI, ANTOINETTA MARIE, GOGRON
  GRO-BOLMOG, TELAENDRIL, M'RAAJ-DAR AND TEINAAVA. ALL OF THESE FAMILY MEMBERS MUST DIE!**"
- **"All of these FAMILY MEMBERS must die."** He uses the word. The Brotherhood's entire identity is that
  they're a family — the Sanctuary calls you Brother or Sister from your first day — and the order is
  phrased in exactly that language.
- The exemptions are a joke that isn't a joke: "**SCHEMER AND THE DARK GUARDIAN ARE ABOVE SUSPICION FOR
  THEIR OWN REASONS.**" **Schemer is a rat.** A literal rat, M'raaj-Dar's pet. The rat is cleared. The
  people are not (cf. our faction web + conscience system + entities).
### STAGE 3 — HE HANDS YOU THE TOOLS, AND THEY'RE MEMENTOS
- "**HE WILL ALSO PROVIDE YOU WITH TWO ITEMS THAT YOU CAN USE ON YOUR TARGETS: A POISONED APPLE AND A SUMMON
  RUFIO'S GHOST SCROLL.**"
- The scroll "**SUMMONS THE SPIRIT OF RUFIO, YOUR VERY FIRST TARGET ON THE INITIATION QUEST A KNIFE IN THE
  DARK.**"
- Your first kill, the old man in the basement you murdered to get INTO this family, comes back as a
  summonable weapon to help you kill the family. That is a sick joke and it's beautiful (cf. our
  AMALGAMATION + death-math + [READ]; the first victim returns as a tool).
- And the apples: "**THERE IS ALSO A BARREL IN LUCIEN LACHANCE'S ROOM IN FORT FARRAGUT WITH TEN POISONED
  APPLES IN IT WITH A VERY HARD LEVELED LOCK.**" He keeps ten. He always keeps ten.
### STAGE 4 — THE METHODS (the game lets you be as cowardly as you want)
- **The apples:** "**PLACING ONE ON A PLATE IN FRONT OF A MEMBER SUCH AS OCHEEVA.**" To make it work you
  must first "**TAKE ALL THE FOOD IN THE SANCTUARY AND PICKPOCKET ALL THE FOOD FROM THEIR INVENTORY.**"
  - You starve your family first so they'll eat the poison. Read that again.
- **The guards:** "**MAKE THEM ANGRY AND THEY WILL FOLLOW AND ATTACK. LEAD THEM THROUGH THE ABANDONED HOUSE
  INTO CHEYDINHAL, WHERE THE GUARDS WILL PURSUE AND KILL THEM.**"
  - You can insult your friends until they chase you, then run into the street and let the CITY GUARD do it.
    You don't even swing (STARTLINGLY our conscience system + faction web + standing; laundering a murder
    through the state).
- **The allergy:** "**VICENTE VALTIERI IS ALLERGIC TO GARLIC. REVERSE-PICK POCKETING SOME INTO HIS INVENTORY
  WILL MAKE HIM EXTREMELY WEAK.**" The vampire who mentored you, killed with a spice, because you know his
  weakness — **because he told you.**
- **The sleeping:** "**YOU CAN STAB THEM WHILE ASLEEP OR WITHIN THE PRIVACY OF THEIR QUARTERS.**"
### STAGE 5 — TELAENDRIL ISN'T HOME (the one who has a life)
- "**TELAENDRIL SPENDS MOST OF HER TIME OUTSIDE THE SANCTUARY, AND ONLY COMES BACK ON MIDDAS.**" "**GETTING
  RID OF TELAENDRIL MAY BE TRICKY, AS SHE IS ABSENT FROM THE CITY ON LOREDAS AND SUNDAS.**"
- The solutions are grotesque in their casualness. **"You can wait until Middas, when she returns to the
  Cheydinhal Sanctuary"** — sit in the house full of your family's corpses and wait for the last one to come
  home. Or: "**ONE WAY TO DETERMINE HER WHEREABOUTS IS LOOTING OCHEEVA'S CORPSE AND ACQUIRING TELAENDRIL'S
  NOTE.**"
- **You loot your dead boss for a note that tells you where the last one is.** Or, the wiki notes flatly:
  "**IT IS ALSO POSSIBLE FOR TELAENDRIL TO GET KILLED BY BANDITS WHILE TRAVELING.**" She might just die on
  the road and save you the trip (cf. our scheduler + entities + [READ] + death-math).
### STAGE 6 — THE ONES WHO FIGHT BACK
- "**THE ONLY PEOPLE WHO MAY GIVE YOU TROUBLE ARE GOGRON GRO-BOLMOG, TEINAAVA, AND VICENTE. TEINAAVA AND
  VICENTE HAVE GOOD SWORDS AND GOGRON WEARS HEAVY ARMOR AND CARRIES AN ENCHANTED BATTLE AXE.**"
- Gogron is the sweetest character in the Sanctuary — an Orc who genuinely loves you and talks about how
  much he likes killing in the most cheerful voice in the game. He's also the hardest fight, because he's
  the best equipped. His affection and his armor are the same fact.
- And the detail: "**IF YOU ARE SEEN KILLING ANY OF THE SANCTUARY RESIDENTS, THE PEOPLE WHO SEE YOU
  (INCLUDING THE DARK GUARDIAN) WILL LOSE 70 DISPOSITION POINTS.**" Being seen murdering their sister makes
  them like you 70 points less. The game models the betrayal as **a number going down** (cf. our standing +
  conscience system).
### STAGE 7 — THE REWARD IS A HORSE AND A PROMOTION
- "**AFTER DEALING WITH ALL OF YOUR ONCE BROTHERS AND SISTERS, RETURN TO FORT FARRAGUT AND SPEAK TO LUCIEN TO
  REQUEST YOUR REWARD (SHADOWMERE, PROMOTION TO SILENCER).**"
- "**SHADOWMERE IS UNARGUABLY THE BEST HORSE IN THE GAME.**" You get an excellent horse.
- "**AS YOU HAVE BECOME A SILENCER, YOU ARE PERMANENTLY EXEMPT FROM FOLLOWING THE FIVE TENETS.**" The
  Brotherhood's sacred laws — including *never betray the Brotherhood* — no longer apply to you. You have
  been promoted out of morality.
- "**HE TELLS YOU THAT YOU WILL NO LONGER BE RECEIVING YOUR CONTRACTS FACE-TO-FACE BUT INSTEAD FROM LETTERS
  IN 'DEAD DROPS.'**" Your reward for killing everyone you knew is that you never have to speak to anyone
  again. **Your job is now a hollow rock** (STARTLINGLY our AMALGAMATION + NETWORK + [READ]; the promotion
  into isolation).
### STAGE 8 — AND THEN IT WAS ALL FOR NOTHING
- Bellamont — the actual traitor, a **Speaker**, not on your list, not in your sanctuary — keeps going. He
  kills "**HALF THE BLACK HAND, INCLUDING LACHANCE HIMSELF.**"
- Lucien, the man who ordered the Purification, is murdered by the traitor the Purification was supposed to
  catch. You find his body.
- "**CHEYDINHAL SANCTUARY WAS LATER RE-ESTABLISHED BY SPEAKER ARQUEN AFTER THE TRAITOR WAS REVEALED.**"
- They rebuilt it. New people. Same rooms. The seven you killed were a rounding error in a filing error (cf.
  our faction web + AMALGAMATION + Megaton law; the institution that survives its own atrocity and reopens).
- And his motive: he "**SOUGHT VENGEANCE FOR THE BROTHERHOOD'S KILLING OF HIS MOTHER MANY YEARS PRIOR.**"
  The whole massacre traces back to a child whose mother the Brotherhood killed on a contract. **The family
  business ate itself** (cf. our fold + generational persistence + AMALGAMATION).

## 2. THE HONEST FLAWS (banked)
===============================================================================
- THERE IS NO REFUSAL: "**REGARDLESS OF ANY RELUCTANCE ON YOUR PART, THERE IS NO WAY AROUND IT; YOU MUST
  KILL YOUR FORMER ALLIES IF YOU WISH TO PROGRESS IN THE DARK BROTHERHOOD QUESTLINE.**" The most morally
  loaded order in the game has no dialogue option to say no, no option to warn them, no option to go after
  Bellamont. LESSON (hard, ours): if the quest's whole subject is obedience, the game MUST let you refuse
  and lose something — otherwise it's not a moral test, it's a cutscene with combat.
- THE GAME DOESN'T LET YOU TELL THEM: the letter says don't speak to anyone about it, and mechanically you
  CAN'T. Seven characters who trust you, and no dialogue tree contains the truth. LESSON: the tragedy is
  bigger if warning them is POSSIBLE and costly.
- THE VICTIMS DON'T REACT LIKE FAMILY: they fight back generically. There's no line where Ocheeva asks why.
  Gogron doesn't say anything about it. Compare Whodunit's guests, who have opinions. LESSON (ours, sharp):
  if you're going to make the player kill people who love them, WRITE THE MOMENT THEY REALIZE — one line
  each is enough, and its absence is the quest's biggest miss.
- THE EXPLOITS ARE FUNNIER THAN THE TRAGEDY: luring your family into the street so the **city guard** kills
  them, or waiting for Telaendril to be "**KILLED BY BANDITS WHILE TRAVELING,**" turns a massacre into a
  physics puzzle. LESSON (double-edged): systemic freedom in a scripted tragedy lets players opt out of the
  feeling entirely.
- THE REWARD IS A GOOD HORSE: Shadowmere and a rank. The emotional weight and the payout are in unrelated
  currencies. LESSON (ours): if your atrocity's reward is a mount, you've told the player what it was worth.
- THE REVEAL IS TOO LATE AND TOO SMALL: Bellamont's identity lands quests later, and the game never makes
  you sit in the fact that the seven were innocent. LESSON: the reckoning has to be a SCENE, not a lore
  entry.
- THE BUG: "**IT IS POSSIBLE YOU WILL NOT RECEIVE A QUEST UPDATE AFTER READING OCHEEVA'S ORDERS, RENDERING
  YOU UNABLE TO ADVANCE IN THE QUEST. SIMPLY RELOADING YOUR PREVIOUS SAVE CAN FIX THIS ISSUE.**" LESSON:
  regression gates.

**LUCIEN LACHANCE** — wants the Cheydinhal sanctuary dead on suspicion of ONE traitor among seven.
