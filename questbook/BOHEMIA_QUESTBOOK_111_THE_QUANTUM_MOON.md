# BOHEMIA QUESTBOOK — DEEP DIVE 111: THE PLACE THAT ONLY EXISTS WHILE YOU LOOK AT IT / SHE IS PRETTY SURE SHE IS DEAD (The Quantum Moon, Outer Wilds)
Full teardown, the whole enchilada: the quest with no key, no combat, no unlock and no item — where the only
thing standing between you and the sixth location is **whether you understand the rules**, taught to you by
a dead civilization in three towers scattered across a solar system, and the reward is a conversation with a
woman who is technically deceased and delighted to meet you. Complete event flow, the imaging rule, the
photograph, the shrine, the North Pole problem, Solanum, the honest flaws, and Bohemia ports. STARTLINGLY
relevant to our [READ], our recorded-vs-unrecorded, our AMALGAMATION, our quest factory, our PACIFIST LAW, +
our despair-with-a-thread-of-hope. Mobius Digital. Reference only; Paolo does not read it. No Bohemia quest
written here.

Quest: the Quantum Moon pilgrimage, Outer Wilds (2019). The Moon "**RANDOMLY PHASES BETWEEN SIX POSITIONS IN
THE SOLAR SYSTEM WHEN NOT BEING OBSERVED. IT CAN EXIST IN ORBIT AROUND THE HOURGLASS TWINS, TIMBER HEARTH,
BRITTLE HOLLOW, GIANT'S DEEP, THE OUTSIDE OF DARK BRAMBLE, AND THE EYE OF THE UNIVERSE.**" You are trying to
stand on it.

## 0. THE CORE IDEA (why it's in the canon)
===============================================================================
- THE ONLY LOCKED DOOR IS YOUR UNDERSTANDING (the thesis — STARTLINGLY our [READ] + quest factory): there is
  no key item. There is no skill check. There is no gear. **You cannot land on the Quantum Moon until you
  personally know a rule**, and once you know it, you could have done it in hour one.
  - The game's entire progression is knowledge in the PLAYER'S head, not flags in a save file. A guide
    author's stance says it all: "**I'VE GONE TO GREAT LENGTHS TO LEAVE ANY ANSWERS ABOUT THE END OF THE
    GAME ALL THE WAY OUT OF THIS GUIDE. **THE GAME IS MADE TO TEACH THE PLAYER HOW TO FIND THEIR OWN
    ANSWERS**, AND I FEEL VERY STRONGLY THAT BY GIVING THOSE ANSWERS TO YOU, I'D BE UNDERMINING THE DEVS AND
    RUINING THE PAYOFF THEY WORKED SO HARD TO DELIVER**" (STARTLINGLY our [READ] + recorded-vs-unrecorded +
    quest factory; progression as literacy).
- THE DEAD BUILT SCHOOLS, NOT DUNGEONS (the design — direct for our AMALGAMATION): the Nomai are extinct.
  What they left behind isn't loot — it's **curriculum.** "**THE SHARDS, AS WELL AS SEVERAL NOMAI STRUCTURES
  DEDICATED TO QUANTUM MOON RESEARCH AND PILGRIMAGES, TEACH DIFFERENT RULES OF QUANTUM BEHAVIOUR WHICH PROVE
  KEY TO REACHING THE MOON ITSELF, AND EVENTUALLY THE MOON'S SIXTH LOCATION.**"
  - The **Tower of Quantum Trials**: "**THE TRIALS THEMSELVES ARE INCREASINGLY DIFFICULT PUZZLES THAT
    INTRODUCE AND TEACH THE LESSON OF 'QUANTUM IMAGING'... THE FINAL ROOM CONTAINS CONGRATULATIONS, AND A
    REMINDER THAT THE OTHER QUANTUM SHARDS HAVE THEIR OWN LESSONS TO TEACH.**"
  - **A dead species left you a graded course with a congratulations room at the end.** They're all gone.
    The classroom still works (STARTLINGLY our AMALGAMATION + [READ] + recorded-vs-unrecorded + district;
    the archive that teaches instead of tells).
- AND THE ANSWER IS THAT YOU HAVE TO STOP LOOKING (the rule that runs the whole thing): "**A CONCRETE FACT
  ABOUT HOW QUANTUM OBJECTS WORK: **THEY TURN THINGS AROUND THEM QUANTUM - INCLUDING YOU, IF YOU'RE NOT
  LOOKING.**"
  - To travel, you must be **unobserved.** To land, you must be **observing.** The same rule is the lock and
    the key depending on which side you want (cf. our [READ] + PACIFIST LAW + AMALGAMATION).

## 1. THE FULL EVENT FLOW (stage by stage)
===============================================================================

### STAGE 0 — YOU FALL THROUGH IT
- "**ATTEMPTING TO LAND ON THE MOON CONVENTIONALLY WILL CAUSE THE PLAYER TO PASS THROUGH IT ENTIRELY,
  MYSTERIOUSLY EMERGING FROM THE CLOUD LAYER BACK INTO SPACE, THE MOON ITSELF LIKELY HAVING DISAPPEARED
  ALREADY.**"
- The failure is silent and physical. You fly at a moon and **come out the other side into empty space.** No
  error message, no "you need an item." The moon simply wasn't there while you were inside its clouds
  (STARTLINGLY our district + quest factory + [READ]; the failure that IS the clue).
### STAGE 1 — THE TOWER IN THE HURRICANE (the on-ramp is a crash)
- "**IF YOU GO INSIDE ONE OF THE SWIRLING TORNADOES, YOU WILL BE SPAT OUT INTO THE GALAXY. BUT, CRASHING YOUR
  SHIP INTO THE BIGGEST OF THEM WILL GET YOU INSIDE THE TOWER OF QUANTUM TRIALS.**"
- The classroom is at the **eye of a hurricane** on an ocean planet, and the way in is to fly your ship into
  the storm on purpose. The Nomai put their school somewhere that filters for commitment (cf. our district +
  VEGAS GEOGRAPHY + MAP LAW).
### STAGE 2 — LESSON ONE: IT MOVES WHEN YOU BLINK
- "**HERE'S WHERE YOU'LL LEARN THAT QUANTUM OBJECTS MOVE WHEN YOU DON'T LOOK AT THEM. YOU PROBABLY FOUND THE
  WANDERING ARCH, BUT IT KEPT SLIPPING AWAY. **THE SOLUTION IS RELATIVELY STRAIGHTFORWARD: KEEP YOUR EYE ON
  IT AS YOU WALK TOWARD IT.**"
- The first puzzle in the tower has been solvable since minute one of the game. You just didn't know the
  rule. **The lock was in you** (cf. our [READ] + recorded-vs-unrecorded).
### STAGE 3 — LESSON TWO: A PHOTOGRAPH COUNTS AS LOOKING
- The Tower's actual curriculum: "**THE RULE THAT OBSERVING AN IMAGE OF AN OBJECT IS EQUIVALENT TO OBSERVING
  THE OBJECT ITSELF. THE PUZZLES REVOLVE AROUND THIS RULE, REQUIRING ONE TO USE THE SCOUT LAUNCHER TO 'LOCK'
  QUANTUM OBJECTS IN PLACE BY TAKING AND OBSERVING PHOTOS OF THEM.**"
- And there it is — the answer to the moon: "**TO PROPERLY LAND ON THE QUANTUM MOON, **A PHOTO OF THE MOON
  MUST BE TAKEN BEFORE ENTERING THE ATMOSPHERE, AND OBSERVED DURING LANDING**, PREVENTING THE MOON FROM
  DISAPPEARING AND ALLOWING LANDING ON ITS SURFACE.**"
- "**BY TAKING A PICTURE OF THE QUANTUM MOON WITH THE SHUTTLE SCOUT, YOU CAN FLY TO IT WITHOUT LOSING SIGHT
  OF IT IN THE CLOUDS. **JUST DON'T USE THE LANDING CAMERA.**"
- Punk: **you land on an impossible moon by photographing it and staring at the photo while you descend
  blind.** The record IS the observation. That is our [READ]'s single best expression in the medium
  (STARTLINGLY our [READ] + recorded-vs-unrecorded + AMALGAMATION; the document that holds reality in place).
### STAGE 4 — THE MOON IS WEARING THE PLANET
- "**THE QUANTUM MOON'S SURFACE IS UNIQUE IN THAT IT WILL LOOK DIFFERENT DEPENDING ON THE PLANET IT ORBITS,
  RESEMBLING AND GAINING ITS FEATURES (THEREFORE HAVING SIX POSSIBLE VARIATIONS). **ALL OBJECTS ON THE
  MOON'S SURFACE WILL ALSO DISPLAY QUANTUM PROPERTIES, MOVING WHEN UNOBSERVED.**"
- "**THE PLAYER WILL ALWAYS LAND AT THE MOON'S SOUTH POLE, REGARDLESS OF THE ORIGINAL ENTRY VECTOR.**"
- You arrive at the south pole. Always. And the terrain rearranges itself the second you turn around (cf. our
  district + HD tile repo + prefab factory; the level that is six levels wearing each other).
### STAGE 5 — THE CORPSE (you meet her dead first)
- On the surface: "**A DEAD NOMAI CORPSE BELONGING TO SOLANUM**."
- A guide, offhand: "**THE FIRST THING YOU'LL LIKELY NOTICE (NOT COUNTING SOLANUM'S MUMMIFIED REMAINS) IS
  THAT THE MOON MIRRORS THE PLANET IT ORBITS.**"
- **You find her body long before you find her.** The mummy is landmark furniture for hours (STARTLINGLY our
  death-math + [READ] + AMALGAMATION + fold; meeting the dead before you meet the person).
### STAGE 6 — THE SHRINE (you make yourself quantum by turning off the light)
- The precept: "**THEY TURN THINGS AROUND THEM QUANTUM - INCLUDING YOU, IF YOU'RE NOT LOOKING. **THIS GIVES
  YOU YOUR ANSWER: YOU CAN 'NOT LOOK' AT THE QUANTUM MOON BY CLOSING THE DOOR OF THE QUANTUM SHRINE WITH THE
  LIGHT OFF, AND IT WILL CARRY YOU TO ANOTHER PLANET.**"
- You walk into a stone hut, shut the door, **switch off your lamp**, and stand in the dark. When you open
  the door, you're at another planet. **You teleported by refusing to witness yourself.**
- "**WHEN THE PLANET CHANGES, THE MOON'S LAYOUT CHANGES, TOO, AND YOU CAN WORK YOUR WAY STEADILY
  NORTHWARD**" (cf. our [READ] + PACIFIST LAW + ART PHILOSOPHY; travel by unobservation).
### STAGE 7 — THE NORTH POLE PROBLEM (the puzzle is a geography problem across six worlds)
- The final rule: "**AT THE TOWER OF QUANTUM KNOWLEDGE ON BRITTLE HOLLOW, IT IS TAUGHT THAT TO REACH THE
  MOON'S SIXTH LOCATION, **THE QUANTUM SHRINE MUST BE USED WHILE ON THE MOON'S NORTH POLE.**"
- And the trap: "**ATTEMPTING TO REACH THE SIXTH LOCATION WHILE THE SHRINE IS AT ANY OTHER LOCATION ON THE
  MOON WILL RESULT IN THE SHRINE'S DOOR BEING COMPLETELY BLOCKED BY OBSIDIAN STONE... **UNABLE TO BE
  EXITED.**"
- Fail this and you are **sealed inside a stone hut** until the sun explodes. The punishment for
  misunderstanding is being walled in alive.
- The actual solve: "**THE MOON'S NORTH POLE IS OFTEN BLOCKED BY DIFFERENT TERRAIN FEATURES IN THE MOON'S
  FIVE OTHER LOCATIONS, REQUIRING A PROCESS THAT INVOLVES USING THE SHRINE MULTIPLE TIMES AT DIFFERENT
  POSITIONS, IN DIFFERENT MOON LOCATIONS, TO REACH A PATH WHERE THE NORTH POLE CAN BE ACCESSED. THIS CAN BE
  EASILY DONE BY ENTERING THE SHRINE AND GOING TO BRITTLE HOLLOW, GIANT'S DEEP OR DARK BRAMBLE VARIATIONS,
  GOING TO THE NORTHERN HEMISPHERE, THEN SWITCHING TO HOURGLASS TWINS OR TIMBER HEARTH VARIATION, GIVING
  ACCESS TO THE NORTH POLE.**"
- **You walk north on one version of the moon and swap to another version where north is walkable.** You are
  using six planets as one staircase (STARTLINGLY our district + MAP LAW + prefab factory; the level solved
  by editing which level you're in).
### STAGE 8 — THE SIXTH LOCATION (and it's quiet)
- "**THE SIXTH VARIATION OF THE QUANTUM MOON IS HIGHLY ENIGMATIC, AND VASTLY DIFFERENT FROM THE OTHER FIVE
  VARIANTS. THE SURFACE IS MAINLY SMOOTH GROUND COVERED IN PURPLE STRATA, WITH MANY OUTCROPPINGS OF STRANGE,
  CRATER-MARKED OBSIDIAN STONE IN ODD GEOMETRIES, IDENTICAL IN COMPOSITION TO THE QUANTUM SHARDS. AN OPEN
  PATHWAY LEADS FROM THE SHRINE ACROSS THE MOON'S SURFACE ALL THE WAY TO THE SOUTH POLE, WHERE **A
  STILL-LIVING SOLANUM CAN BE FOUND AND CONVERSED WITH, STANDING UNDER A LARGE VORTEX IN THE MOON'S CLOUD
  LAYER THAT LEADS INTO DEEP SPACE.**"
- **She's standing under a hole in the sky.** And you already found her corpse.
- "**AFTER JUMPING AROUND THE GALAXY, YOU EVENTUALLY ARRIVE AT THE 6TH LOCATION OUTSIDE THE EYE OF THE
  UNIVERSE, FOLLOWING THE TWISTED PATH TO DISCOVER A SHOCKING SIGHT: SOLANUM. **THEY ARE THE LAST NOMAI IN
  EXISTENCE.**"
### STAGE 9 — THE CONVERSATION (you have to build the language)
- She can't speak. "**SHE DOESN'T MAKE IT OBVIOUS ENOUGH FOR YOU THROUGH CHARADES? OKAY. YOUR ANSWER: **PICK
  UP ANY COMBINATION OF TWO SPEAKING STONES AND PLACE THEM ON HER PLINTHS, AND SHE WILL WRITE THE ASSOCIATED
  INFORMATION FOR YOU. THE SAME TWO STONES WILL PRODUCE THE SAME ANSWER IN EITHER ORDER.**"
- **You interview the last member of an extinct species by combining nouns on pedestals.** The conversation
  is a crafting system. You assemble your questions from rocks.
- What she tells you: "**THEY EXPLAINED THAT THEY HAVE NO IDEA HOW LONG THEY'VE BEEN ON THE QUANTUM MOON,
  BUT **THEY'RE PRETTY SURE THEY'RE TECHNICALLY DEAD. THEY'RE A QUANTUM VERSION OF THEMSELVES.**"
- **"Pretty sure they're technically dead."** She's not sure. She's fine. She's the last of her kind, alive
  because someone is looking at her, and she is **pleased to have company** (STARTLINGLY our AMALGAMATION +
  death-math + despair-with-a-thread-of-hope; the dead person who is good company).
- The walkthrough's reaction is the correct one: "**THIS PERSON CONSIDERS YOU FRIENDS. WHAT A CUTIE
  PATOOTIE.**"
- And what she knows: "**THE EYE OF THE UNIVERSE APPEARED TO BE THE STARTING POINT OF ALL LIFE AND EXISTED
  BEFORE THE GALAXY'S FORMATION.**"
### STAGE 10 — THE EXIT IS THE PUNCHLINE
- "**ATTEMPTING TO EXIT THE MOON'S ATMOSPHERE THROUGH THE VORTEX WILL PLACE THE PLAYER **NEAR THE SOUTH POLE
  ON THE TIMBER HEARTH VARIATION, ON SOLANUM'S CORPSE.**"
- You leave the living Solanum through the hole in the sky and **land on her dead body.** Both are true. The
  game does not resolve it (cf. our AMALGAMATION + death-math + recorded-vs-unrecorded).

  INVOLVES USING THE SHRINE MULTIPLE TIMES AT DIFFERENT POSITIONS, IN DIFFERENT MOON LOCATIONS.**" Elegant
