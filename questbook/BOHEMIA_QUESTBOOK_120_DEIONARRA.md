# BOHEMIA QUESTBOOK — DEEP DIVE 120: HE MADE HER LOVE HIM SO HER GHOST WOULD BE USEFUL (Deionarra, Planescape: Torment)
Full teardown, the whole enchilada: the quest where you meet a dead woman who loves you, learn from her own
mouth that your previous self courted her, married her in all but name, and killed her on purpose because
he calculated that her grief would anchor her spirit somewhere he needed a scout — and she still calls you
"my Love." Complete event flow, the plan, the prophecy, the ability, the legacy, the honest flaws, and
Bohemia ports. STARTLINGLY relevant to our AMALGAMATION, our fold, our [READ], our conscience system, our
companions, + our ART PHILOSOPHY. Black Isle / Chris Avellone. Reference only; Paolo does not read it. No
Bohemia quest written here.

Quest: the Ghost of Deionarra, Planescape: Torment (1999). You wake on a slab in a mortuary with no memory.
The second person you meet is your widow.

## 1. THE FULL EVENT FLOW (stage by stage)
===============================================================================

### STAGE 0 — SHE OPENS WITH RAGE AND THEN IMMEDIATELY FORGIVES YOU
- "**AS I WATCHED, SHE STIRRED SLIGHTLY, AND HER EYES FLICKERED... SHE LOOKED AROUND SLOWLY, THEN SAW ME.
  **HER TRANQUIL FACE SUDDENLY TWISTED INTO A SNARL. 'YOU! WHAT IS IT THAT BRINGS *YOU* HERE?! HAVE YOU COME
  TO SEE FIRST-HAND THE MISERY YOU HAVE WROUGHT? PERHAPS IN DEATH I STILL HOLD SOME SHRED OF USE FOR
  YOU...?'** HER VOICE DROPPED TO A HISS. **'...MY LOVE.'**"
- **"Perhaps in death I still hold some shred of use for you."** She knows. She's known the whole time she's
  been dead. And she says it as sarcasm — **she doesn't know how right she is** (STARTLINGLY our conscience
  system + AMALGAMATION + ART PHILOSOPHY).
- And then: "**IN A SUDDEN CHANGE OF EMOTION, THE SPIRIT MADE... '*REMEMBER* ME, MY LOVE?' THE GHOST
  STRETCHED OUT HER ARMS. 'THINK...' HER VOICE BECAME DESPERATE AGAIN. **'...THE NAME *DEIONARRA* MUST EVOKE
  SOME MEMORY WITHIN YOU.'**"
- The rage lasts one line. **Then she's begging.** (cf. our companions + fold + despair-with-a-thread-of-hope).
### STAGE 1 — THE FIRST DIALOGUE OPTION IS A LIE OR A CRUELTY
- The wiki's alignment note: "**EVIL & CHAOTIC: **LYING, SAYING HER NAME SOUNDS FAMILIAR.**"
- And the walkthrough's optimal path is exactly that lie: "**ASK HER WHO SHE IS, **CLAIM THAT YOU FEEL THE
  STIRRINGS OF A MEMORY**, AND THEN ASK IF SHE KNOWS WHO YOU ARE, AND CONTINUE TALKING ABOUT YOURSELF UNTIL
  YOU GET THE RAISE DEAD ABILITY AND 1000 EXP.**"
- **The efficient route through your dead wife is to lie to her about remembering her.** The guide says it
  as a step (STARTLINGLY our conscience system + [READ] + no-clean-answer; the optimal play is the cruelty).
### STAGE 2 — SHE DIAGNOSES YOU BETTER THAN ANYONE IN THE GAME
- "**'DO YOU KNOW WHO I AM?' 'YOU ARE ONE BOTH BLESSED AND CURSED, MY LOVE. AND YOU ARE ONE WHO IS NEVER FAR
  FROM MY THOUGHTS AND HEART.'**"
- "**'THE NATURE OF YOUR CURSE SHOULD BE APPARENT, MY LOVE. LOOK AT YOU.' SHE POINTED AT ME. **'DEATH REJECTS
  YOU. YOUR MEMORIES HAVE ABANDONED YOU. DO YOU NOT PAUSE AND WONDER WHY?'**"
- And then the single scariest paragraph in the game: "**'I DO BELIEVE THAT **EVERY INCARNATION WEAKENS YOUR
  THOUGHTS AND MEMORIES.** YOU CLAIM YOU HAVE LOST YOUR MEMORY. PERHAPS IT IS A SIDE EFFECT OF COUNTLESS

- **The dead woman explains your own respawn mechanic to you, and the explanation is that immortality is
  eating you.** The narration's response: "**I WONDERED HOW MANY TIMES I HAD AWOKEN IN THIS MORTUARY**"

### STAGE 3 — AND SHE TELLS YOU WHAT HE PROMISED HER

## 2. THE HONEST FLAWS (banked)
===============================================================================
- THE BEST SCENE IN THE GAME IS BEHIND A STAT CHECK: "**WITH AT LEAST 11 CHARISMA OR 12 INTELLIGENCE, THE
  '"MY LOVE"? DO I KNOW YOU?' AND 'I THINK I CAN FEEL THE STIRRINGS OF MY MEMORY...' OPTIONS WILL APPEAR.**"
  LESSON (~9th confirmation, ours): **gating your thesis behind a build means most players never see it** —
  our [READ] needs a FLOOR. A low-INT run meets his widow and has nothing to say to her.
- THE WALKTHROUGH REDUCES HER TO AN XP DISPENSER: "**YOU CAN GET 1000 EXP FROM DEIONARRA FOR REGAINING YOUR
  RAISE DEAD ABILITY (ASK HER ABOUT YOURSELF), YOU ALSO GET 500 EXP FOR HAVING HER TELL YOU ABOUT THE PORTAL
  OUT OF THE MORTUARY.**" LESSON (double-edged, ours): if your emotional climax also pays the best XP in the
  zone, the guide will describe it as a farm — and thousands of players will run it as one.
- THE PORTAL INFO IS REDUNDANT: "**YOU'LL GET 500 EXP FOR HER ANSWER ON HOW TO LEAVE (**WHICH IS THE SAME
  WAY AS VAXIS' WAY TO LEAVE**).**" You can get the same information from a random Dustman by lying to him
  instead. LESSON: if the emotionally expensive route and the free route give the same output, the
  expensive one is optional — see the ~16th theme-in-optional confirmation.
- SHE CANNOT BE HELPED AND THE GAME NEVER OFFERS: no quest to free her, no resolution, no release. LESSON
  (double-edged, and correct here): a character you cannot fix is honest AND players will spend 20 years
  writing mods to fix her.
- HER ENTIRE TRAGEDY IS BACKSTORY DELIVERED BY WIKI: the Practical Incarnation's plan is assembled from
  journals and a late-game confrontation. Most first-time players finish the mortuary thinking she's just a
  sad ghost. LESSON (~16th confirmation): theme-in-optional is missed — hide it in the city, never behind a
  lock (Yakuza Q114).
- THE OPTIMAL PLAY IS THE EVIL OPTION: "**EVIL & CHAOTIC: LYING, SAYING HER NAME SOUNDS FAMILIAR**" is
  literally the walkthrough's recommended line. LESSON (ours, ~4th confirmation): if the efficient path is
  the cruel one, players take it and never register it as a choice.

## V2-A CAST + WHAT EACH ONE WANTS

**DEIONARRA** — dead, in love, and USED: your previous incarnation engineered her love and her death so her grief would anchor a scout where he needed one. Wants: you — knowing, now, most of what "you" means. Will trade: the prophecy (the game's whole plot, which she cannot stop saying), a power (her legacy ability), and the favor at the Eternal Boundary. Will never say: that she regrets it — and that refusal is the horror. FUNCTION: the ghost as INFRASTRUCTURE — a person converted to equipment who still calls the converter "my Love."

**THE PRACTICAL INCARNATION (the previous you)** — wanted a scout in the Fortress; priced a woman's afterlife and paid it. FUNCTION: the predecessor-as-author — the dynast-succession nightmare: what if the LAST you was the villain of THIS you's quest. (The Fold's darkest port lives here.)

**THE PLAYER (the Nameless One, current)** — wants answers; inherits debts denominated in other people's eternities. FUNCTION: the heir who must ask a favor of the person his estate destroyed.
